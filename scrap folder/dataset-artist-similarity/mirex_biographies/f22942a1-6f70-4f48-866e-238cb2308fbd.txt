**Aphex Twin**, born [Richard David James](http://www.last.fm/music/Richard+David+James), August 18, 1971, in Limerick, Ireland to Welsh parents Lorna and Derek James, is an electronic music artist. He grew up in Cornwall, United Kingdom and started producing music around the age of 12 and made his first release aired on 1985.       Richard has been hailed as “the most inventive and influential figure in contemporary [electronic](http://www.last.fm/tag/electronic) music”, with his works ranging from [ambient](http://www.last.fm/tag/ambient) pieces to [acid techno](http://www.last.fm/tag/acid%20techno).   In 1991 Aphex formed the [Rephlex](http://www.last.fm/label/Rephlex) record label with his friend Grant Wilson-Claridge. Signed artists include Mike Paradinas (aka [µ-Ziq](http://www.last.fm/music/%C2%B5-Ziq)) and [Squarepusher](http://www.last.fm/music/Squarepusher).      Also known as: [AFX](http://www.last.fm/music/AFX), [Blue Calx](http://www.last.fm/music/Blue+Calx), [Bradley Strider](http://www.last.fm/music/Bradley+Strider), [Caustic Window](http://www.last.fm/music/Caustic+Window), [The Dice Man](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Dice+Man), [GAK](http://www.last.fm/music/GAK), [Polygon Window](http://www.last.fm/music/Polygon+Window), [Power- Pill](http://www.last.fm/music/Power-Pill), [Q-Chastic](http://www.last.fm/music/Q-Chastic), [Soit-P.P.](http://www.last.fm/music/Soit-P.P.), Rich in [Mike & Rich](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+&+Rich), [Martin Tressider](http://www.last.fm/music/Martin+Tressider) in [Universal Indicator](http://www.last.fm/music/Universal+Indicator)…      Also rumoured to be, but has never been proven, part of [The Tuss](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Tuss).
