**The Prodigy** are a Braintree, Essex, UK, [electronic](http://www.last.fm/tag/electronic) music group (aka **[Prodigy](http://www.last.fm/music/Prodigy)**), formed by **[Liam Howlett](http://www.last.fm/music/Liam+Howlett)** (composition, keyboards), in 1990. They have sold over 20 million records worldwide, which is almost unequalled in dance music history.      Their music consists of various styles ranging from [rave](http://www.last.fm/tag/rave) and [breakbeat hardcore](http://www.last.fm/tag/breakbeat%20hardcore) in the early [90s](http://www.last.fm/tag/90s) to [alternative rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/alternative%20rock) and [big beat](http://www.last.fm/tag/big%20beat), with [punk](http://www.last.fm/tag/punk) vocal elements in later times. Other current band members include: **[Keith Flint](http://www.last.fm/music/Keith+Flint)** (dancing, vocals), **[Maxim](http://www.last.fm/music/Maxim)** (MCing, vocals) and live guitarist **Rob Holliday** ([Marilyn Manson](http://www.last.fm/music/Marilyn+Manson), [Gary Numan](http://www.last.fm/music/Gary+Numan), [Curve](http://www.last.fm/music/Curve), [Sulpher](http://www.last.fm/music/Sulpher)).      **[Leeroy Thornhill](http://www.last.fm/music/Leeroy+Thornhill)** (dancing, occasional live keyboards), was a member of the band from 1990 to 2000, as well was a female dancer / vocalist **[Sharky](http://www.last.fm/music/Sharky)**, who left the band during their early period. Other notable former live members include: **Gizz Butt** (guitar - [English Dogs](http://www.last.fm/music/English+Dogs), [Janus Stark](http://www.last.fm/music/Janus+Stark), [Sabbat](http://www.last.fm/music/Sabbat)) - toured with the band for three years throughout the late ’90s, **Kieron Pepper** (drum - [RealTV](http://www.last.fm/music/RealTV), [Happy Gilmore](http://www.last.fm/music/Happy+Gilmore)) - played for the group from 1997 to 2007 (also contributing to the solo projects of **Maxim** and **Flint**’s band, [Flint](http://www.last.fm/music/Flint)), as well as **[Jim Davies](http://www.last.fm/music/Jim+Davies)** (guitar - [Pitchshifter](http://www.last.fm/music/Pitchshifter)), who also performed in Flint.      **The Prodigy** first emerged on the underground [rave](http://www.last.fm/tag/rave) scene in the early 1990, releasing their début LP **_[Experience](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Prodigy/Experience)_** (1992) and have since then achieved immense popularity and worldwide renown.
