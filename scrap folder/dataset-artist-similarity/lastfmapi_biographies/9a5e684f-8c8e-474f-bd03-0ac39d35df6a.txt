Bluvertigo is an Italian [alternative rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/alternative%20rock) band.      The band formed in 1992. Former members were [Morgan](http://www.last.fm/music/Morgan) (Marco Castoldi), [Andy](http://www.last.fm/music/Andy) (Andrea Fumagalli) and Marco Pancaldi. In 1994 Sergio Carnevale joined the band, while in 1996 Pancaldi was substituted by Livio Magnini.      Their first album is [Acidi E Basi](http://www.last.fm/music/Bluvertigo/Acidi+E+Basi) (1995), followed by [Metallo Non Metallo](http://www.last.fm/music/Bluvertigo/Metallo+Non+Metallo) (1997) and [Zero](http://www.last.fm/music/Bluvertigo/Zero) (1999), the latest studio album of the band. In 2001 was published Pop Tools, a greatest hits compilation.
