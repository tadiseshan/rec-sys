**Candie Payne** is a female singer born in Liverpool, UK but her formative years were mostly spent in New York, USA before returning to Liverpool in the early 90’s. She is the sister of [The Zutons](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Zutons) drummer, Sean Payne and former [The Stands](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Stands) frontman [Howie Payne](http://www.last.fm/music/Howie+Payne).      Signed to Sony imprint, Deltasonic, in 2006 she released her debut album, the heavily 60’s-influenced _[I Wish I Could Have Loved You More](http://www.last. fm/music/Candie+Payne/I+Wish+I+Could+Have+Loved+You+More)_ in May 2007. The album was produced and co-written by Simon Dine of [Noonday Underground](http://www.last.fm/music/Noonday+Underground).
