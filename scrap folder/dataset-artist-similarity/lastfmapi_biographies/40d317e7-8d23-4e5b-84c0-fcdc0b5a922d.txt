There are multiple artists with this name:      Black Milk, is a [hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip%20hop) producer and MC from Detroit, Michigan.      In 2004 he formed [B.R. Gunna](http://www.last.fm/music/B.R.+Gunna) with Young RJ, and Fat Ray, and together they released [Dirty District: Vol. 2](http://www.last.fm/music/B.R.+Gunna/Dirty+District:+Vol.+2), a follow up to a compilation released in 2001 by [Slum Village](http://www.last.fm/music/Slum+Village), which he had contributed production to. Black Milk released a solo album, [Sound of the City](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/Sound+of+the+City), the following year and in 2006 he signed a record contract with [Fat Beats Records](http://www.last.fm/label/Fat+Beats+Records). In the fall of 2006, he released an EP, entitled [Broken Wax](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/Broken+Wax). March 13, 2007 he released a second album, [Popular Demand](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/Popular+Demand).      In addition to Slum Village, Black Milk has worked with the likes of [J Dilla](http://www.last.fm/music/J+Dilla), [Elzhi](http://www.last.fm/music/Elzhi), [Phat Kat](http://www.last.fm/music/Phat+Kat), [Frank-N-Dank](http://www.last.fm/music/Frank-N-Dank), [Lloyd Banks](http://www.last.fm/music/Lloyd+Banks), [Canibus](http://www.last.fm/music/Canibus), and [Pharoahe Monch](http://www.last.fm/music/Pharoahe+Monch), and handled most of the production on [T3](http://www.last.fm/music/T3)’s Olio mixtape of 2006.      In the Winter of 2007/2008 Black Milk paired with [Aftermath](http://www.last.fm/label/Aftermath) recording artist [Bishop Lamont](http://www.last.fm/music/Bishop+Lamont) to release a mixtape entitled [Caltroit](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/Caltroit), which was nominated for Best Hip Hop Mixtape at Justo’s Mixtape Awards.      Black Milk released an album with [Fat Ray](http://www.last.fm/music/Fat+Ray) entitled [The Set Up](http://www.last.fm/music/Fat+Ray+&+Black+Milk/The+Set+Up) on March 4, 2008. His second solo album [Tronic](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/Tronic) was released on October 28, 2008. He also handled the majority of production on Elzhi’s album [The Preface](http://www.last.fm/music/Elzhi/The+Preface), released August 12 2008.      On December 25, 2009, he leaked the first single from his album [Album of the Year](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/Album+of+the+Year), the song is called [Keep Going](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/_/Keep+Going). He has also released two more singles named [Welcome (Gotta Go)](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/_/Welcome+\(Gotta+Go\)) and [Deadly Medley](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk/_/Deadly+Medley) featuring [Royce da 5’9”](http://www.last.fm/music/Royce+da+5%279%22) and Elzhi.      2) Black Milk is a [gothic rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/gothic%20rock)/[altern ative](http://www.last.fm/tag/alternative) band from Nordrhein-Westfalen, Germany. They released the album “Ultrawide” in 2003
