Jeff Loomis was the lead guitarist for the ground breaking metal band [Nevermore](http://www.last.fm/music/Nevermore). A player of many influences and styles, Loomis is often seen as one of the world’s most talented and gifted metal guitarists. In 2008, he released a solo album entitled ‘Zero Order Phase’ (Century Media), featuring special guest appearances by fellow guitarists [Ron Jarzombek](http://www.last.fm/music/Ron+Jarzombek) and Pat O’Brien, legendary jazz bassist [Michael Manring](http://www.last.fm/music/Michael+Manring), and OFZ producer Neil Kernon.
