Lisa Ekdahl (born 29th July 1971 in Stockholm) is a Swedish singer and song writer in popular music and jazz singer. Debut in 1990 with Peter Nordahls jazz trio and contributes to an album with Toni Holgersson. Contract with EMI. The self named debut album, Lisa Ekdahl was released in 1994 She has so far published ten albums, six of them in Swedish and four in English.
