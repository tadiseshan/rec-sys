**Travis Landon Barker**, (born November 14, 1975), is the current drummer for San Diego-based [blink-182](http://www.last.fm/music/blink-182) and [+44](http://www.last.fm/music/%252B44). Travis has also played drums for [Box Car Racer](http://www.last.fm/music/Box+Car+Racer), [Transplants](http://www.last.fm/music/Transplants), [The Aquabats](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Aquabats) (as Baron Von Tito) and is 1/3 in hiphop group [Expensive Taste](http://www.last.fm/music/Expensive+Taste) with Transplants bandmate [Rob Aston](http://www.last.fm/music/Rob+Aston). He also was half of [TRV$DJAM](http://www.last.fm/music/TRV%24DJAM) before DJ AMâ€™s death.      According to [Songfacts](http://www.songfacts.com/detail.php?id=22175), Barker released his first solo album, [Give The Drummer Some](http://www.last.fm/music/Travis+Barker/Give+The+Drummer+Some) on March 15, 2011. The title was based on something [James Brown](http://www.last.fm/music/James+Brown) used to say on stage when he would break it down and let the drummer take over.      He has also worked with [Queens of the Stone Age](http://www.last.fm/music/Queens+of+the+Stone+Age) (as a session drummer on a few of their tracks), [Avril Lavigne](http://www.last.fm/music/Avril+Lavigne) (as a guest drummer),[The Vandals](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Vandals), and [The Suicide Machines](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Suicide+Machines).      Travis Barker is also known for his brand Famous Stars and Straps and is also involved with Rouge Status, DTA and a number of other clothing companies in California.
