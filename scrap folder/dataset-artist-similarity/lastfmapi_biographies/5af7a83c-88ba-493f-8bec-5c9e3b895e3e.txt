**Trilok Gurtu** (born in Mumbai (Bombay) on 30 October 1951) is an Indian percussionist. His mother, singer [Shobha Gurtu](http://www.last.fm/music/Shobha+Gurtu), started him on the tabla, and he later studied the instrument with [Abdul Karim](http://www.last.fm/music/Abdul+Karim). He started playing the Western drum kit in the 1970s, and became interested in jazz. In the 1970s he played with Oregon,[Charlie Mariano](http://www.last.fm/music/Charlie+Mariano), [John Tchicai](http://www.last.fm/music/John+Tchicai), [Terje Rypdal](http://www.last.fm/music/Terje+Rypdal), and [Don Cherry](http://www.last.fm/music/Don+Cherry).      In the 1980s he played with [John McLaughlin](http://www.last.fm/music/John+McLaughlin) and since the 1990s he has been a solo artist, sometimes with a band called Crazy Saints. He co- created an album with [Robert Miles](http://www.last.fm/music/Robert+Miles) entitled _Miles Gurtu_.
