Katharine Hope McPhee (born March 25, 1984) is a [pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/pop) singer, songwriter, actress, model, and television personality from Los Angeles. She gained fame as a contestant on American Idol in 2006, finishing as the runner-up. Her Idol single, [Somewhere Over The Rainbow / My Destiny](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/Somew here+Over+The+Rainbow+%2F+My+Destiny), was released June 27, 2006 through RCA Records. “[Somewhere Over The Rainbow](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPh ee/_/Somewhere+Over+The+Rainbow)” peaked at #12 on the Billboard Hot 100 and “[My Destiny](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/_/My+Destiny)” peaked at #60. 32 weeks following its release, the single had climbed to #4 on the Billboard Hot Singles Sales charts. It was the second highest bestselling single of 2006 after [Taylor Hicks](http://www.last.fm/music/Taylor+Hicks)’ “[Do I Make You Proud](http://www.last.fm/music/Taylor+Hicks/_/Do+I+Make+You+Proud).” The single remained on the chart for more than 58 weeks.      McPhee’s [self-titled debut album](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/Katharine+McPhee) was released through RCA Records on January 30, 2007 and debuted at #2 on the Billboard 200. It also sold 116,000 copies in its first week. The album’s first single, “[Over It](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/_/Over+It),” was a Pop Top 30 hit and certified gold in 2008. Its second single, “[Love Story](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/_/Love+Story),” was released May 22, 2007, but failed to chart. On January 11, 2008, it was reported by Billboard.com that McPhee had been released from her RCA Records contract; a spokesperson for the label stated, “She is going to record her next album on her own.” She ended up signing a new record deal with Verve Forecast Records (part of the Verve Music Group under Universal Music Group), which was announced on January 27, 2009.      McPhee’s second album, [Unbroken](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/Unbroken) was released through [Verve Forecast](http://www.last.fm/label/Verve+Forecast) on January 5, 2010 and debuted at #27 on the Billboard 200. The album’s first single was “[Had It All](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+McPhee/_/Had+It+All)” which peaked at #22 on the Billboard AC chart. She also released a holiday album titled [Christmas Is The Time To Say I Love You](http://www.last.fm/music/Kath arine+McPhee/Christmas+Is+The+Time+To+Say+I+Love+You) on October 12, 2010. It debuted at #11 on the Billboard Top Holiday Albums chart while the single “[Have Yourself A Merry Little Christmas](http://www.last.fm/music/Katharine+M cPhee/_/Have+Yourself+A+Merry+Little+Christmas)” peaked at #16 on the Billboard AC chart.      McPhee has also established an acting career; she co-starred in The House Bunny in 2008 as the hippie sorority sister Harmony. She will also be co- starring in the upcoming NBC series Smash
