**Brad Mehldau** is an American jazz pianist born on August 23, 1970 in Jacksonville, Florida. He plays original compositions and jazz standards, as well as interpretations of non-jazz songs by [The Beatles](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Beatles), [Nick Drake](http://www.last.fm/music/Nick+Drake), [Radiohead](http://www.last.fm/music/Radiohead) and [Soundgarden](http://www.last.fm/music/Soundgarden), for example. He has also contributed to movie soundtracks, including [Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil](http://www.last.fm/music/Various+Artists/Midnight+in+the+Garden+of+Good+and+Evil) (1997), [Eyes Wide Shut](http://www.last.fm/music/Various+Artists/Eyes+Wide+Shut) (1999), [Space Cowboys](http://www.last.fm/music/Various+Artists/Space+Cowboys) (2000), [The Million Dollar Hotel](http://www.last.fm/music/Various+Artists/The+Million+Dollar+Hotel) (2000).       In 1994 he founded [Brad Mehldau Trio](http://www.last.fm/music/Brad+Mehldau+Trio), with bassist [Larry Grenadier](http://www.last.fm/music/Larry+Grenadier) and drummer [Jorge Rossy](http://www.last.fm/music/Jorge+Rossy); in 2005, [Jeff Ballard](http://www.last.fm/music/Jeff+Ballard) replaced Rossy. Brad Mehldau started playing piano at the age of six, and has released more than ten albums.
