Born Patrick Denard Douthit in Winston-Salem, NC, 9th Wonder is a Grammy Award Winning Producer, DJ, College Lecturer, and Social Activist. Since his introduction to hip-hop in 1982, 9th has been immersed in the music and culture of the art form, while gaining experience in music theory throughout middle and high school. 9th attended North Carolina Central University, where he decided to pursue a career in music. He, along with [Phonte Coleman](http://www.last.fm/music/Phonte+Coleman) and Thomas Jones ([Rapper Big Pooh](http://www.last.fm/music/Rapper+Big+Pooh)), formed the hip-hop trio [Little Brother](http://www.last.fm/music/Little+Brother) in 1998. The group released the critically acclaimed album [The Listening](http://www.last.fm/music/Little+Brother/The+Listening), which received 4 mics in Source Magazine.      9th was then tapped to produce a track on [Jay-Z](http://www.last.fm/music/Jay-Z)’s [Black Album](http://www.last.fm/music/Jay-Z/Black+Album), which became his first major label placement. From there, 9th Wonder produced 3 songs for the R&B Super group [Destiny’s Child](http://www.last.fm/music/Destiny%27s+Child) on the album “[Destiny Fulfilled](http://www.last.fm/music/Destiny%27s+Child/Destiny+Fulfilled) (“[Girl](http://www.last.fm/music/Destiny%27s+Child/_/Girl)”, “[Is She the Reason](http://www.last.fm/music/Destiny%27s+Child/_/Is+She+the+Reason)”, “[Game Over](http://www.last.fm/music/Destiny%27s+Child/_/Game+Over)”), won a Grammy with [Mary J. Blige](http://www.last.fm/music/Mary+J.+Blige) for her album [The Breakthrough](http://www.last.fm/music/Mary+J.+Blige/The+Breakthrough) (“[Good Woman Down](http://www.last.fm/music/Mary+J.+Blige/_/Good+Woman+Down)”), [Erykah Badu](http://www.last.fm/music/Erykah+Badu)’s “[Honey](http://www.last.fm/music/Erykah+Badu/_/Honey)”, and “[20 Feet Tall](http://www.last.fm/music/Erykah+Badu/_/20+Feet+Tall)” on the album [New Amerykah 1 and 2](http://www.last.fm/music/Erykah+Badu/New+Amerykah+1+and+2), [Ludacris](http://www.last.fm/music/Ludacris)’ “[Do The Right Thang](http://www.last.fm/music/Ludacris/_/Do+The+Right+Thang)”, a song featuring [Common](http://www.last.fm/music/Common) and [Spike Lee](http://www.last.fm/music/Spike+Lee), and most recently [David Banner](http://www.last.fm/music/David+Banner) on the album [Death Of A Popsta r](http://www.last.fm/music/David+Banner+&+9th+Wonder/Death+Of+A+Popstar). 9th will also be working with [Drake](http://www.last.fm/music/Drake) for the second time on his sophomore album release. 9th also has 3 albums with [Murs](http://www.last.fm/music/Murs), an Emcee that hails from MidCity, CA, in which all three albums have received critical acclaim.      9th was chosen by Aaron McGruder to score music for the critically acclaimed series “The Boondocks”. He has endorsed companies such as M-Audio Electronics, FL Studio, LRG Clothing Company, and Creative Recreation Shoe Company
