Subsonica is an Italian [electronic](http://www.last.fm/tag/electronic)/[rock] (http://www.last.fm/tag/rock) band.      The group was formed in 1996 in Turin, Italy. Their eponymous debut album debuted at the end of 1996. The band gained real success in 2000 by playing the [Sanremo Music Festival](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Festival_della_canzone_italiana). The first two singles off of the album [Microchip Emozionale](http://www.last.fm/music/Subsonica/Microchip+Emozionale), [Tutti I Miei Sbagli](http://www.last.fm/music/Subsonica/_/Tutti+I+Miei+Sbagli) and [Discoteca Labirinto](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Subsonica/_/Discote ca+Labirinto) were hits. Their latest album, [Eden](http://www.last.fm/music/Subsonica/Eden) was released in 2011.      Subsonica are: [Boosta](http://www.last.fm/music/Boosta) (Davide Di Leo) keyboards, Bass Vicio (Luca Vicini) bass, Samuel (Umberto Romano) vocals, C-Max (Massimiliano Casacci) guitar, Ninja (Enrico Matta) drums.
