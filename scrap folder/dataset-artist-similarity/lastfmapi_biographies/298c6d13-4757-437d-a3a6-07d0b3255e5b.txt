Von Hertzen Brothers is a Finnish [progressive rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/progressive%20rock) band. It consists of three brothers Kie, Mikko and Jonne von Hertzen. On their album [Love Remains the Same](http://www.last.fm/music/Von+Hertzen+Brothers/Love+Remains+the+Same) (2008), Mikko Kaakkurinniemi (drums, percussion) and Juha Kuoppala (piano, keyboards) are listed as part of Von Hertzen Brothers.      Their debut album [Experience](http://www.last.fm/music/Von+Hertzen+Brothers/Experience) was released in 2001 and re-released in 2006. In 2006, they released their second album titled [Approach](http://www.last.fm/music/Von+Hertzen+Brothers/Approach). Their third album, [Love Remains the Same](http://www.last.fm/music/Von+Hertzen+Brothers/Love+Remains+the+Same), came out on the 14th of May 2008 and went straight to #1 on the official Finnish album charts. Their next album [Stars Aligned](http://www.last.fm/music/Von+Hertzen+Brothers/Stars+Aligned) was released on 23. March 2011.      Their 5th album [Nine Lives](http://www.last.fm/music/Von+Hertzen+Brothers/Nine+Lives) was released on March 21st 2013 with an official video for the single [”Flowers And Rust”](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1tAYCTZt6bA)      Official site: [www.VonHertzenBrothers.com](http://www.VonHertzenBrothers.com)
