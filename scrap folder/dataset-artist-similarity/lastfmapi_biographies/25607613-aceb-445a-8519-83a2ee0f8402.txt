[Marc Anthony](http://www.marcanthonyonline.com) (born Marco Antonio Muñiz; September 16, 1968) is a singer-songwriter, actor and producer. According to the Guinness Book of World Records, Anthony is the top selling tropical [salsa](http://www.last.fm/tag/salsa) artist of all time. The five-time Grammy winner has sold more than 10 million albums worldwide. He is best known for his Latin salsa numbers and [ballad](http://www.last.fm/tag/ballad)s. Anthony was the first solo salsa act to ever sell out Madison Square Garden.      In 1991 he released his first album, [When The Night Is Over](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/When+The+Night+Is+Over). His 1995 collection, “[Todo A Su Tiempo](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/Todo+A+Su+Tiempo)”, was nominated for a Grammy with songs like “[Te Conozco Bien](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/_/Te+Conozco+Bien)”, “[Hasta Ayer](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/_/Hasta+Ayer)”, “[Nadie Como Ella](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/_/Nadie+Como+Ella)”, “[Se Me Sigue Olvidando](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/_/Se+Me+Sigue+Olvidando)”, “[Te Amare](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/_/Te+Amare)”, and “[Llegaste A Mi](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Anthony/_/Llegaste+A+Mi)”.      Anthony was born Marco Antonio Muñiz in New York City, US, to Guillermina, a housewife, and Felipe Muñiz, a musician and hospital lunchroom worker.[3] His parents are Puerto Rican and named him after Mexican singer [Marco Antonio Muñiz](http://www.last.fm/music/Marco+Antonio+Mu%C3%B1iz). He has a sister named Yolanda Muñiz. He also has 4 older brothers and 2 additional older sisters, 4 siblings from his mother’s and 2 siblings from his father’s prior marriages.      Marc Anthony married pop superstar [Jennifer Lopez](http://www.last.fm/music/Jennifer+Lopez) on June 5, 2004. On February 22, 2007 they welcomed twins Max David and Emme Marbiel Muñiz-Lopez.
