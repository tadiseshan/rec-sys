Krayzie Bone (born Anthony Henderson on June 17, 1974 and also known as [Leatha Face](http://www.last.fm/music/Leatha+Face)) is a member of the seminal hip-hop group, [Bone Thugs-N-Harmony](http://www.last.fm/music/Bone+Thugs-N-Harmony). He, like his other group members, is known for his tongue-twisting rapid pace of delivery and his ability to create harmonic hooks within songs. He is the most laid back, versatile, and soft spoken member of Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, letting his work show for itself.
