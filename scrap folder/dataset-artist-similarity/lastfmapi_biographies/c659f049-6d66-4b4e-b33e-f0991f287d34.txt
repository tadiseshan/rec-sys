Sean Price (born March 17, 1972) is an American rapper and member of the hip hop supergroups [Boot Camp Clik](http://www.last.fm/music/Boot+Camp+Clik) and [Random Axe](http://www.last.fm/music/Random+Axe) with [Guilty Simpson](http://www.last.fm/music/Guilty+Simpson) and producer [Black Milk](http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Milk). He came to fame as one-half of the duo [Heltah Skeltah](http://www.last.fm/music/Heltah+Skeltah), performing under the name Ruckus, or in short Ruck, along with partner Rock. He also is featured in the video game NBA 2K11 as a playable character in street mode. Price lives in Brownsville, Brooklyn.      Sean Price aka Sean P is an American rapper from Brownsville, Brooklyn, New York, famous as a member of hip-hop collective [Boot Camp Clik](http://www.last.fm/music/Boot+Camp+Clik). He came to fame as a member of the duo [Heltah Skeltah](http://www.last.fm/music/Heltah+Skeltah), performing under the name Ruck, with partner Rock. He has released two albums with Heltah Skeltah, 1996’s acclaimed [Nocturnal](http://www.last.fm/music/Heltah+Skeltah/Nocturnal) and 1998’s [Magnum Force](http://www.last.fm/music/Heltah+Skeltah/Magnum+Force). He released his solo debut in 2005, titled [Monkey Barz](http://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Price/Monkey+Barz). He continues work with the Boot Camp Clik and recently reunited with Rock, with the duo planning to release another Heltah Skeltah album titled _D.I.R.T._, an acronym for Da Incredible Rap Team. Price has recently contributed a track on the upcoming Chinga Chang Records mixtape _Residual Income VOL 1_ entitled “Down Wit My Niggaz”. Sean has also been featured in the hit game Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare, with a snippet of one of his songs in a level. [Jesus Price Supastar](http://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Price/Jesus+Price+Supastar), his sophomore album, was released in early 2007. He is famous for being quoted saying “I’m the brokest rapper alive.” He just released a new Heltah Skeltah album entitled D.I.R.T. (Da Incredible Rap Team) alongside Rock September 30th, 2008
