Brenton Brown, born in South Africa, is a Christian songwriter and worship leader. He was part of Vineyard music (UK), and during this time Brenton has been the worship pastor at Oxford Vineyard, UK, and the coordinator of the Vineyard (UK) Worship Development Team. He has since left Vineyard, having spent some time back home in Cape Town. He lives in Malibu, California with his wife, Jude and is doing a solo career. In 2006 he released his first solo album, Everlasting God.      The title track - Everlasting God - has gained a lot of popularity within the world of worship music. It has been recorded by other artists, including Lincoln Brewster, and most notably by Chris Tomlin on his latest album, “See The Morning”.
