Sophie Michelle Ellis-Bextor (born 10 April 1979) is an English singer, songwriter and model. She first came to prominence in the late 1990s, as the lead singer of the indie rock band [Theaudience](http://www.last.fm/music/Theaudience). After the group disbanded, Ellis-Bextor went solo, achieving widespread success in the early 2000s. Her music is a mixture of mainstream pop, disco, nu-disco, and 1980s electronic influences.      Ellis-Bextor has released five solo albums: [Read My Lips](http://www.last.fm/music/Sophie+Ellis-Bextor/Read+My+Lips), [Shoot from the Hip](http://www.last.fm/music/Sophie+Ellis-Bextor/Shoot+from+the+Hip), [Trip the Light Fantastic](http://www.last.fm/music/Sophie+Ellis- Bextor/Trip+the+Light+Fantastic), [Make a Scene](http://www.last.fm/music/Sophie+Ellis-Bextor/Make+a+Scene) and the most recent, [Wanderlust](http://www.last.fm/music/Sophie+Ellis-Bextor/Wanderlust), which was released on January 20th 2014.
