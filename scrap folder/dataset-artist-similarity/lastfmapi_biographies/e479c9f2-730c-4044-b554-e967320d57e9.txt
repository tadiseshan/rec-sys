**Kathryn Williams** (born in 1974 in Liverpool, England) is a [singer-songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag/singer-songwriter) based in Newcastle, England, whose work is characterised by delicate vocals and acoustic instruments.      Her influences are varied, including [Nina Simone](http://www.last.fm/music/Nina+Simone), [Nick Drake](http://www.last.fm/music/Nick+Drake), [Joni Mitchell](http://www.last.fm/music/Joni+Mitchell), [Bob Dylan](http://www.last.fm/music/Bob+Dylan), [John Lennon](http://www.last.fm/music/John+Lennon), [Simon and Garfunkel](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Simon+and+Garfunkel), and [Velvet Underground](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Velvet+Underground). She has sung with [Badmarsh & Shri](http://www.last.fm/music/Badmarsh+&+Shri) album and worked with Twisted Nerve artist Pedro.      Her album, _[Leave To Remain](http://www.last.fm/music/Kathryn+Williams/Leave+To+Remain)_, was greeted with critical acclaim, and her earlier _[Little Black Numbers](http://www.last.fm/music/Kathryn+Williams/Little+Black+Numbers)_ was nominated for a Grammy award.      In 2010 Williams collaborated with former [Delicate Vomit](http://www.last.fm/music/Delicate+Vomit) member [Anna Spencer](http://www.last.fm/music/Anna+Spencer) as [The Crayonettes](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Crayonettes) on the album [Playing Out - Songs For Children And Robots](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Crayonettes/ Playing+Out+-+Songs+For+Children+And+Robots)      In 2012 she launched a new project called ‘[The Pond](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Pond).’   A trio with [Simon Edwards](http://www.last.fm/music/Simon+Edwards) and [Ginny Clee](http://www.last.fm/music/Ginny+Clee), the project mixed by [Portishead](http://www.last.fm/music/Portishead)’s [Adrian Utley](http://www.last.fm/music/Adrian+Utley).
