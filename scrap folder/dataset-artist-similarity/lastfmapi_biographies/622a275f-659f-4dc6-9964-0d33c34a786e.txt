**Michel Fugain** is a French singer-songwriter who began his songwriting career for others (like [Hugues Aufray](http://www.last.fm/music/Hugues+Aufray) or [Dalida](http://www.last.fm/music/Dalida)) in the early 1960s and released his first record in 1966. He reached the peak of his popularity with his musical troupe “[Big Bazar](http://www.last.fm/music/Big+Bazar)” in the early 1970s, with a string of major hits including “[Une belle histoire](http://www.last.fm/music/Michel+Fugain/_/Une+belle+histoire)” and “[Fais comme l’oiseau](http://www.last.fm/music/Michel+Fugain/_/Fais+comme+l%27oiseau)”, and had never ceased to record since then.
