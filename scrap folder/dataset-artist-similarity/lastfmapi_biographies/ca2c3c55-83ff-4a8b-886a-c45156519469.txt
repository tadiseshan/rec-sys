**Fabri Fibra** (born Fabrizio Tarducci on October 17, 1976) is an italian rapper. He is [Nesly Rice](http://www.last.fm/music/Nesly+Rice)’s brother.      He recorded his first demotape when he was 17, in 1995. In 1996 records [album]Dei Di Mare Quest’el Gruv[/album] together with the DJ and beatmaker [Lato](http://www.last.fm/music/Lato), followed by [album]Rapimento Del Vulpla[/album] (1997).      In 1999 Fabri Fibra and Lato recorded [album]Sindrome Di Fine Millennio[/album], using the pseudonym of [Uomini Di Mare](http://www.last.fm/music/Uomini+Di+Mare), featuring [El Presidente](http://www.last.fm/music/El+Presidente) (also known as [Esa](http://www.last.fm/music/Esa)), [Inoki](http://www.last.fm/music/Inoki), [Joe Cassano](http://www.last.fm/music/Joe+Cassano) and his brother Nesly Rice. This album was relatively successfull on the italian scene.
