**Lou Barlow** (born Louis Knox Barlow on July 17, 1966, in Dayton, Ohio) is an American alternative rock musician and songwriter. A founding member of the groups [Deep Wound](http://www.last.fm/music/Deep+Wound), [Dinosaur Jr.](http://www.last.fm/music/Dinosaur+Jr.), [Sebadoh](http://www.last.fm/music/Sebadoh), and [The Folk Implosion](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Folk+Implosion), Barlow, from a base in Western Mass-Boston in 1982, is credited with helping to pioneer the lo-fi style of rock music in the late 1980s and early 1990s.      Though born in Dayton, Ohio, Barlow was raised in Jackson, Michigan and then Westfield, Massachusetts. He is widely considered to be among the most prolific and ground-breaking [indie](http://www.last.fm/tag/indie) musicians of the 1990’s. He’s best known for his work with [Sebadoh](http://www.last.fm/music/Sebadoh), [The Folk Implosion](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Folk+Implosion) and [Dinosaur Jr.](http://www.last.fm/music/Dinosaur+Jr.)’s original and current lineup. Many labels have been applied to his unique brand of [acoustic rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/acoustic%20rock), including [lo- fi](http://www.last.fm/tag/lo-fi), [folk-pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/folk-pop) and [folkcore](http://www.last.fm/tag/folkcore). Barlow’s latest album, his second official solo effort under his own name, is titled [Goodnight Unknown](http://www.last.fm/music/Lou+Barlow/Goodnight+Unknown).
