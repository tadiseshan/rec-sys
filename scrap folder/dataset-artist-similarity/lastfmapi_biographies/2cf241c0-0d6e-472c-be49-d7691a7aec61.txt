Daniela Mercury (born Daniela Mercuri de Almeida on July 28, 1965), is a Brazilian [axe](http://www.last.fm/tag/axe), [samba- reggae](http://www.last.fm/tag/samba-reggae), and [mpb](http://www.last.fm/tag/mpb) singer, songwriter, and [record producer](http://www.last.fm/tag/record%20producer). Since her breakthrough, Mercury has become one of the best known Brazilian female singers, selling over 20 million albums worldwide. She is also the Brazilian female performer with most #1 hits in the country, with 14.
