Articolo 31 is a popular band in [Italy](http://www.last.fm/place/Italy), melding [hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip%20hop), [funk](http://www.last.fm/tag/funk) and traditional [italian](http://www.last.fm/tag/italian) musical forms. They are one of the most popular Italian [rap](http://www.last.fm/tag/rap) groups.      **Band history**   Members are rapper [J Ax](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/J+Ax) and DJ Jad; their producer is Franco Godi who produced the music for the Signor Rossi animated series. They took their name from the Irish constitutional law guaranteeing freedom of the press.   They moved from pure [hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip%20hop) to hip hop with many influences from [rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/rock) (sampling [Bob Dylan](http://www.last.fm/music/Bob+Dylan)), [klezmer](http://www.last.fm/tag/klezmer) (sampling the [Klezmatics](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Klezmatics)) etc. to a rock /pop-inspired kind of rap.   With “[Domani Smetto](http://www.last.fm/music/Articolo+31/_/Domani+Smetto)” they left the world of hip hop per se, rapping and singing to rock/pop tracks. They worked together with [Kurtis Blow](http://www.last.fm/music/Kurtis+Blow) on the album “[Xché Si!](http://www.last.fm/music/Articolo+31/Xch%C3%A9+Si!)”, which also included an electro track.
