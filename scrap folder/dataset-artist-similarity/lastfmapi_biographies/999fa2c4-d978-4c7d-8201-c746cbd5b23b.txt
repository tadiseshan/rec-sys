[Greg Laswell](http://www.last.fm/music/Greg+Laswell) is an alternative singer/songwriter from San Diego, California. Laswell was the lead singer of the Escondido, CA based alternative rock band [Shillglen](http://www.last.fm/music/Shillglen). In 2001, they released their first and only album [Sometimes I Feel](http://www.last.fm/music/Shillglen/Sometimes+I+Feel). After Shillglen’s breakup, Laswell released his first solo album in 2003, the critically acclaimed [Good Movie](http://www.last.fm/music/Greg+Laswell/Good+Movie). In 2004, Laswell won the San Diego Music Award for Best Local Recording and was later nominated for 2005’s Best Alternative Artist. Laswell’s follow up album [Through Toledo](http://www.last.fm/music/Greg+Laswell/Through+Toledo) was released on July 11, 2006 through Vanguard Records. Spring 2008 Laswell was touring across Europe on the final leg of the Hotel Cafe 2008 Tour with fellow Hotel Cafe artists; Cary Brothers, Brian Wright, Catherine Feeny, Tom McRae and Jim Bianco. July 8, 2008 he released [Three Flights from Alto Nido](http://www.last.fm/music/Greg+Laswell/Three+Flights+from+Alto+Nido).      Laswell also heads up the production company 20 Inch Records. His recent production credits include albums by artists such as [Molly Jenson](http://www.last.fm/music/Molly+Jenson), [The Derren Raser Band](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Derren+Raser+Band), and [Dustin Shey](http://www.last.fm/music/Dustin+Shey).
