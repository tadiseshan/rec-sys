Pelle Miljoona (b. February 10, 1955 as Petri Tiili) is a Finnish punk rock musician who assembled his first band in 1977. He is the most well-known punk musician in Finland.      Most of the time, he sings and plays the drums, but he’s also known to play the guitar. His biggest hit is “Moottoritie on kuuma” with his band Pelle Miljoona Oy from 1980.      Pelle Miljoona has also written several novels and poetry books.
