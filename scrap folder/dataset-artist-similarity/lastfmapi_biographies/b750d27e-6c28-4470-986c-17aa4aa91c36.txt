**Fredrika Stahl** (born October 24, 1984) is a [jazz pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz%20pop) [singer-songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag/singer-songwriter) from [Stockholm](http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&source=s_q&hl=en&geocode=&q=Stockholm,+Sweden&sll=59.332788,18.064488&sspn=0.249708,0.617294&g=Stockholm,+Sweden&ie=UTF8&hq=&hnear=Stockholm,+Sweden&ll=57.562995,20.698242&spn=34.233849,79.013672&z=4), [Sweden](http://www.last.fm/place/Sweden).      Her debut album, **_[A Fraction Of You](http://www.last.fm/music/Fredrika+Stahl/A+Fraction+Of+You)_**, was released 27 March [2006](http://www.last.fm/tag/2006) in [France](http://www.last.fm/place/France) and in her native [Sweden](http://www.last.fm/place/Sweden) and [Finland](http://www.last.fm/place/Finland) on 14 June [2006](http://www.last.fm/tag/2006). It features musicians such as [Tom McClung](http://www.last.fm/music/Tom+McClung) on piano and arrangements, José Palmer on guitar, [Diego Imbert](http://www.last.fm/music/Diego+Imbert) on double bass and [Karl Jannuska](http://www.last.fm/music/Karl+Jannuska) on drums. Fredrika wrote the lyrics and music.   Her second album, **_[Tributaries](http://www.last.fm/music/Fredrika+Stahl/Tributaries)_**, was released 7 May [2008](http://www.last.fm/tag/2008) in [France](http://www.last.fm/place/France), [Sweden](http://www.last.fm/place/Sweden) and [Germany](http://www.last.fm/place/Germany). It was also released in [Japan](http://www.last.fm/place/Japan), [Poland](http://www.last.fm/place/Poland), the [Russian Federation](http://www.last.fm/place/Russian+Federation) and South Korea. It features [Hiro Morozumi](http://www.last.fm/music/Hiro+Morozumi) on piano, [Øyvind Nypan](http://www.last.fm/music/%C3%98yvind+Nypan) and [Andreas Öberg](http://www.last.fm/music/Andreas+%C3%96berg) on guitar, [Pierre Boussaguet](http://www.last.fm/music/Pierre+Boussaguet) and Acelio de Paula on bass and Simoné Prattico on drums as well as a large array of Parisian horn and string musicians.
