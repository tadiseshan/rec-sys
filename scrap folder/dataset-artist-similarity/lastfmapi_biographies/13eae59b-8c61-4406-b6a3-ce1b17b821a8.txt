Trey Gunn, American born December 13, 1960. In addition to his seven solo recordings, ten-string touch-guitarist, **Trey Gunn** has co-created, toured and/or recorded with [King Crimson](http://www.last.fm/music/King+Crimson), [David Sylvian](http://www.last.fm/music/David+Sylvian), [Vernon Reid](http://www.last.fm/music/Vernon+Reid), [Brian Eno](http://www.last.fm/music/Brian+Eno), [Tool](http://www.last.fm/music/Tool), [Azam Ali](http://www.last.fm/music/Azam+Ali), [Puscifer](http://www.last.fm/music/Puscifer), [John Paul Jones](http://www.last.fm/music/John+Paul+Jones), [Robert Fripp](http://www.last.fm/music/Robert+Fripp), David Hykes of The Harmonic Choir, Matt Chamberlain and Punishment Farm. He is currently working with [KTU](http://www.last.fm/music/KTU), his multi-dimensional performance group [Quodia](http://www.last.fm/music/Quodia), a duo with Pat Mastelotto [TU](http://www.last.fm/music/TU) and is scoring for film and television.
