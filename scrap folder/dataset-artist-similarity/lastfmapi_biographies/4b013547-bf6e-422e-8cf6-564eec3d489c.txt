Fantan Mojah is a rising reggae star, he hails from Kingston but was born Owen Moncrieffe in St. Elizabeth, Jamaica. Inspired by artist like Bob Marley, Burning Spear, Buju Banton, Capleton and Sizzla. Fantan Mojah has alsways been very motivated by music, he also is a strong religious man. Those two factors combined is Fantan Mojah, it is how he makes his powerful Reggae/Dancehall music. He signed with Greensleeves Records and in November 2005 Fantan Mojah released his debut album ‘Hail The King’, containing the No. 1 hit single ‘Hungry’.
