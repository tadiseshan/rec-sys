Pauline Croze (born on May 4,1979 in Noisy-le-Sec, France) is a pop/folk artist, often associated with the New French Scene (Nouvelle Scène Française). She started singing and playing the guitar at the age of 14.      In 2003, she appeared in the ‘Festival des Transmusicales de Rennes” and was revealed to the public. She has opened for Miossec, Tryo and Cali. Her eponymous first album came out in early 2005.
