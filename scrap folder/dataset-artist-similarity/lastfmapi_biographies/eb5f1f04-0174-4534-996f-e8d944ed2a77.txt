Ida Maria (full name Ida Maria Børli Sivertsen, b. July 13th 1984) is an indie [rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/rock)/[punk](http://www.last.fm/tag/punk) artist from Nesna, [Norway](http://www.last.fm/place/Norway), currently living in [Sweden](http://www.last.fm/place/Sweden). Her first album, [Fortress Round My Heart](http://www.last.fm/music/Ida+Maria/Fortress+Round+My+Heart), was released in Norway and the UK on May 5th, 2008.      Ida Maria won Zoom Urørt in 2006 and the Urørt-competition in 2007 (both are Norwegian competitions for unsigned artists) and played some of Norway`s biggest festivals. In 2007 she also won Alarmprisen (Norwegian music awards).
