Harold Faltermeyer (born October 5, 1952 in Munich) is a German musician, keyboardist, [composer](http://www.last.fm/tag/composer) and record producer.      He is recognized as one of the composers/producers who best captured the zeitgeist of 19[80s](http://www.last.fm/tag/80s) [synth- pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/synth-pop) in film scores. Best known for the “Axel F” electronic theme for “Beverly Hills Cop” and the “Top Gun Anthem” from the [soundtrack](http://www.last.fm/tag/soundtrack) for “Top Gun” - both often imitated, highly influential [instrumental](http://www.last.fm/tag/instrumental) hits that to some extent practically redefined action film scoring in the [80s](http://www.last.fm/tag/80s).      As a session musician, arranger and producer, Faltermeyer has worked with several international pop stars including [Donna Summer](http://www.last.fm/music/Donna+Summer), [Pet Shop Boys](http://www.last.fm/music/Pet+Shop+Boys), [Blondie](http://www.last.fm/music/Blondie), [Amanda Lear](http://www.last.fm/music/Amanda+Lear), [Patti LaBelle](http://www.last.fm/music/Patti+LaBelle), [Barbra Streisand](http://www.last.fm/music/Barbra+Streisand), [Glenn Frey](http://www.last.fm/music/Glenn+Frey), [Laura Branigan](http://www.last.fm/music/Laura+Branigan), [Billy Idol](http://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Idol), [Jennifer Rush](http://www.last.fm/music/Jennifer+Rush), [Alexis](http://www.last.fm/music/Alexis), [Cheap Trick](http://www.last.fm/music/Cheap+Trick), [Sparks](http://www.last.fm/music/Sparks), [Bob Seger](http://www.last.fm/music/Bob+Seger), [Chris Thompson](http://www.last.fm/music/Chris+Thompson), and [Bonnie Tyler](http://www.last.fm/music/Bonnie+Tyler) .      He has won two Grammy Awards: the first in 1986 for Best Album of original [score](http://www.last.fm/tag/score) written for a motion picture or television special, as a co-writer of the “Beverly Hills Cop” [soundtrack](http://www.last.fm/tag/soundtrack); and the second in 1987 for Best Pop Instrumental Performance with guitarist [Steve Stevens](http://www.last.fm/music/Steve+Stevens) for “Top Gun Anthem” from the [soundtrack](http://www.last.fm/tag/soundtrack).      The highly recognizable “Axel F” theme was recorded using three instruments: a Roland Jupiter-8, a Roland JX-3P and a Yamaha DX7 and also a LinnDrum drum machine. It has been covered by numerous artists and in May 2005 a re- recording of the classic reached number one in the UK singles chart after being remixed with the [Crazy Frog](http://www.last.fm/music/Crazy+Frog) ringtone.      “After Tango & Cash I made a decision to go back and raise my children in Germany, where I was born”, Faltermeyer said in a 2006 interview for the “Tango & Cash” [soundtrack](http://www.last.fm/tag/soundtrack) CD. From his Red Deer Studios Estate in Munich, Germany, he has continued producing hit records and soundtracks mainly for the German market.
