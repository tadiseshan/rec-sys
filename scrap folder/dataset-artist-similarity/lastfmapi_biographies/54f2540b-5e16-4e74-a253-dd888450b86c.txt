Renan Luce (born on the 5 March 1980, Paris) is a French singer-songwriter. Born in Paris, Luce spent all his childhood and adolescence in Plourin-les- Morlaix, Brittany. He is married to Lolita Sechan, the daughter of famous French singer [Renaud](http://www.last.fm/music/Renaud). His debuted in 2006, releasing his first album _Repenti_ within the European francophone countries and a year later in Canada. In 2009 his second album _Le clan des miros_ was released.
