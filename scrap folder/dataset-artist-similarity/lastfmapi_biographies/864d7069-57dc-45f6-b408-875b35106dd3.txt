Singer/Songwriter [Colin Hay ](http://www.last.fm/music/colin+hay) is the former front-man of the classic Australian band **[Men at Work](http://www.last.fm/music/men+at+work)**. Since the group disbanded he has released several solo albums, including _[Man at Work](http://www.last.fm/music/Colin+Hay/Man+at+Work)_, which featured re- recordings of classic **Men at Work** songs in an acoustic environment.      More recently Hay has been involved with the NBC TV series **[ Scrubs](http://www.scrubs-tv.com/)**, lending several songs to the sitcom, including “[Waiting for My Real Life to Begin](http://www.last.fm/music/Colin+ Hay/_/Waiting+for+My+Real+Life+to+Begin),” “[My Brilliant Feat](http://www.last.fm/music/colin+hay/_/My+Brilliant+Feat)” and “[Beautiful World](http://www.last.fm/music/colin+hay/_/Beautiful+world).” He also starred as a troubadour in the first episode of season two, “[My Overkill](http://www .scrubs-tv.com/ep25.html),” performing his song “[Overkill](http://www.last.fm/music/colin+hay/_/overkill),” which was also the basis for the episode’s title. he is also a top dog
