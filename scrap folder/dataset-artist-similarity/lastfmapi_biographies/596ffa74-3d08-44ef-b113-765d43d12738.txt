Justin Randall Timberlake (born January 31, 1981 in Memphis, Tennessee) is a Grammy Award winning singer, Emmy Award winning actor, producer and dancer.      At a young age of 4, Timberlake already had a love for music. Joining singing competitions as early as age 7, he was coached by well-renowned child coach Bob Westbrook. After an appearance on Star Search, he continued his dreams, beating out thousands of kids to join the cast of [Disney](http://www.last.fm/label/Disney)’s Mickey Mouse Club. At age 15, he rose to commercial fame as one of the frontmen of the hugely successful pop group [*NSYNC](http://www.last.fm/music/*NSYNC) in the late 90’s that debut in 1995. The group was a sensation, making a place for themselves in the music world with a succession of top-selling albums and singles like [Bye Bye Bye](http://www.last.fm/music/*NSYNC/_/Bye+Bye+Bye) and [This I Promise You](http://www.last.fm/music/*NSYNC/_/This+I+Promise+You). He released his debut solo album [Justified](http://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Timberlake/Justified) in 2002, cranking out hits like [Rock Your Body](http://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Timberlake/_/Rock+Your+Body), [Like I Love You](http://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Timberlake/_/Like+I+Love+You) and [Cry Me a River](http://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Timberlake/_/Cry+Me+a+River). It was certified 4 times platinum by the [riaa](http://www.last.fm/tag/riaa), with Timberlake winning Best Pop Vocal Album and Best Male Pop Vocal Performance at the 45th Grammy Awards. After a string of sold-out world arena tours, 40 million records sold (in addition to his 55 million albums sold with [*NSYNC](http://www.last.fm/music/*NSYNC)), 3 #1 singles, awards from the AMAs, VMAs, BRITs, World Music Awards, EMAs, VH1 Quadruple Threat recipient, Timberlake solidified his position as a pop superstar. Timberlake’s work has earned him six Grammy Awards and four Emmy Awards.      Aside from music, he also has a thriving acting career, with leading roles in movies like [Alpha Dog](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alpha_Dog), [In Time](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/In_Time), [Black Snake Moan](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Snake_Moan_\(film\)), [Friends With Benefits](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Friends_with_Benefits_\(film\)) and the Oscar-nominated film [The Social Network](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Social_Network). On October 19, [2012](http://www.last.fm/tag/2012) he married longtime girlfriend, actress [Jessica Biel](http://www.last.fm/music/Jessica+Biel) at the Borgo Egnazia resort in Fasano, [Italy](http://www.last.fm/place/Italy).      He is a five-time host on the popular sketch comedy show, [Saturday Night Live](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saturday_Night_Live). His other ventures include record label Tennman Records, fashion label William Rast, and the restaurants Destino and Southern Hospitality.
