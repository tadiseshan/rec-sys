Corey Isiah Christie, better known by his stage name Reks is a Boston rapper. His albums [Along Came The Chosen](http://www.last.fm/music/Reks/Along+Came+The+Chosen) (2001, [Landspeed Records](http://www.last.fm/label/Landspeed+Records)) and [Grey Hairs](http://www.last.fm/music/Reks/Grey+Hairs) (2008, [Showoff Records](http://www.last.fm/label/Showoff+Records)) were both released to general acclaim in the [hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip%20hop) community. His debut featured [hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip%20hop) musicians including [Young Zee](http://www.last.fm/music/Young+Zee), [ED OG](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/ED+OG), [J-Live](http://www.last.fm/music/J-Live) and [7L](http://www.last.fm/music/7L) while his second album featured [DJ Premier](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Premier), [Large Professor](http://www.last.fm/music/Large+Professor), [Skyzoo](http://www.last.fm/music/Skyzoo), [Big Shug](http://www.last.fm/music/Big+Shug), [Termanology](http://www.last.fm/music/Termanology), [Krumbsnatcha](http://www.last.fm/music/Krumbsnatcha) and was mostly produced by [Statik Selektah](http://www.last.fm/music/Statik+Selektah). His 2008 song, [Say Goodnight](http://www.last.fm/music/Reks/_/Say+Goodnight) was #3 on about.com’s list of the top 100 [rap](http://www.last.fm/tag/rap) songs of 2008. His most recent album is [R.E.K.S.](http://www.last.fm/music/Reks/R.E.K.S.), which stands for Rhythmatic Eternal King Supreme, and was released to critical acclaim on March 12, 2011. In 2012 Reks appeared on UK Hip Hop artist [Ricta](http://www.last.fm/music/Ricta)’s debut album [Snacks & Deception](http://www.last.fm/music/Ricta/Snacks+&+Deception).
