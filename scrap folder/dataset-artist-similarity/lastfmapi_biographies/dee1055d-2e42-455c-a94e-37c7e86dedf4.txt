Hell Razah is a [Wu-Tang Clan](http://www.last.fm/music/Wu-Tang+Clan) affiliated Brooklyn-born MC and a founding member of the [Sunz of Man](http://www.last.fm/music/Sunz+of+Man).      Razah made his rap debut in the early 90s as a founding member of the [Sunz of Man](http://www.last.fm/music/Sunz+of+Man) along with [Killah Priest](http://www.last.fm/music/Killah+Priest), [Shabazz the Disciple](http://www.last.fm/music/Shabazz+the+Disciple), [Prodigal Sunn](http://www.last.fm/music/Prodigal+Sunn), [Supreme](http://www.last.fm/music/Supreme), [7th Ambassador](http://www.last.fm/music/7th+Ambassador) and [60 Second Assassin](http://www.last.fm/music/60+Second+Assassin). The group have released three albums to date (“The Last Shall Be First”, “The First Testament” and “Saviorz Day”)      As a solo artist, Razah has released a slew of mixtapes (most of which are available online) aswell as five studio albums - When All Hell Breaks Loose (2001, re-released in 2008), Freedom of Speech (2004, with [4th Disciple](http://www.last.fm/music/4th+Disciple)), Renaissance Child (2007), Razah’s Ladder (2007, with [Blue Sky Black Death](http://www.last.fm/music/Blue+Sky+Black+Death)) and his most recent album Heaven Razah (2010).      Razah is also a member the group [T.H.U.G. Angelz](http://www.last.fm/music/T.H.U.G.+Angelz), with [Shabazz the Disciple](http://www.last.fm/music/Shabazz+the+Disciple) and have released one album “Welcome To Red Hook Houses” in 2008. The group have since started Twitter beefing and are now in hiatus.
