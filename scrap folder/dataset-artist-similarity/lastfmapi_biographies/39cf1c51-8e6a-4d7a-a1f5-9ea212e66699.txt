Plastic Bertrand is the recording name of Roger Jouret, a singer from Brussels, Belguim. Bertrand is best known for the 1977 single “[Ça plane pour moi](http://www.last.fm/music/Plastic+Bertrand/_/%C3%87a+plane+pour+moi)” which was released one month after [Elton Motello](http://www.last.fm/music/Elton+Motello)’s “[Jet Boy, Jet Girl](http://www.last.fm/music/Elton+Motello/_/Jet+Boy,+Jet+Girl)”, which has the same backing track.      While credited to Bertrand, The original recording “Ça plane pour moi” was sung by the song’s producer, [Lou Deprijck](http://www.last.fm/music/Lou+Deprijck). Bertrand has released ten albums since 1977 and represented Luxembourg in the 1987 Eurovision Song Contest.
