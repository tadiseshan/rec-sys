People Under the Stairs is an [underground hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/underground%20hip%20hop) duo which formed in 1997 in Los Angeles, California, United States. The duo consists of Thes One and Double K. They have released eight albums: “The Next Step” (1998), “Question in the Form of an Answer” (2000), “O.S.T.” (2002), “…Or Stay Tuned” (2003), “Stepfather” (2006), “Fun DMC” (2008), “Carried Away” (2009) and “Highlighter” (2011).
