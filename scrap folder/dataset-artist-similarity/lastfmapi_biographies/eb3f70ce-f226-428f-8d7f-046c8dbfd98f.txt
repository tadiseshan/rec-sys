**Junkie XL** (also known as JXL) is musician [Tom Holkenborg](http://www.last.fm/music/Tom+Holkenborg), born in Lichtenvoorde, The Netherlands on 8 December 1967. After producing and touring with the Dutch group [Weekend at Waikiki](http://www.last.fm/music/Weekend+at+Waikiki), together with [Phil Mills](http://www.last.fm/music/Phil+Mills) he founded the industrial band [Nerve](http://www.last.fm/music/Nerve) and released 2 albums (1993 and 1995). After he and [Chris Hinze](http://www.last.fm/music/Chris+Hinze) under name [A Forest Called Mulu](http://www.last.fm/music/A+Forest+Called+Mulu) recorded trip-hop/ambient album and began writing his own tracks as Junkie XL in 1997. He has worked with many [electronic music](http://www.last.fm/tag/electronic%20music) artists, including [Sander Kleinenberg](http://www.last.fm/music/Sander+Kleinenberg), [DJ Tiësto](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/DJ+Ti%C3%ABsto) and [Sasha](http://www.last.fm/music/Sasha).      In 2002 he left The Netherlands to live and work in the [United States](http://www.last.fm/place/United+States) where he works on soundtracks for movies and computer games.      He uses the name “JXL” in those cases where the term Junkie might cause offense.
