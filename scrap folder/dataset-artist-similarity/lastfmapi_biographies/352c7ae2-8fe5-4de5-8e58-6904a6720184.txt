Marco Sfogli (born April 4, 1980) is an Italian Guitarist, songwriter, arranger and session player.   At the young age of 25 Marco was invited to track guitars for James Labrie’s 2005 Solo album titled ‘Elements of Persuasion’.   His main influences are John Petrucci and Andy Timmons, however his guitar playing skills cover pretty much all fields of music, from Prog Rock/Metal, Virtuoso soloing, Fusion, Blue grass, Pop and much much more.   Marcos Solo album was released February 2008.
