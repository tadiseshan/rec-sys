[Enrico Rava](http://www.last.fm/music/Enrico+Rava) is an Italian trumpeter who recorded for such labels as [ECM](http://www.last.fm/label/ECM), [Label Bleu](http://www.last.fm/label/Label+Bleu), Soulnote and [Philology](http://www.last.fm/label/Philology).      Ravaâ€™s career started at an early age, when he played in clubs in Turin. From the beginning, he was a great admirer of [Miles Davis](http://www.last.fm/music/Miles+Davis) and [Chet Baker](http://www.last.fm/music/Chet+Baker). During those years, Rava met [Don Cherry](http://www.last.fm/music/Don+Cherry), [Mal Waldron](http://www.last.fm/music/Mal+Waldron) and [Steve Lacy](http://www.last.fm/music/Steve+Lacy), with whom he played free jazz in a quartet, appearing alternately between London and Buenos Aires.      As of late 2008, Rava plays with his new quintet [Enrico Rava Quintet](http://www.last.fm/music/Enrico+Rava+Quintet), which is composed of such young talents as [Gianluca Petrella](http://www.last.fm/music/Gianluca+Petrella) and [Stefano Bollani](http://www.last.fm/music/Stefano+Bollani) as well as established musicians [Rosario Bonaccorso](http://www.last.fm/music/Rosario+Bonaccorso) and [Roberto Gatto](http://www.last.fm/music/Roberto+Gatto).
