MC Eiht (born Aaron Tyler; May 22, 1967 in Compton, California) is one of the pioneers of [gangsta rap](http://www.last.fm/tag/gangsta%20rap) with ties to the Crips street gang. He first emerged as one of the lead rappers of [Compton’s Most Wanted](http://www.last.fm/music/Compton%27s+Most+Wanted) (CMW). He then became an actor and solo MC. He is best known in the acting world for his role as “A-Wax” in The Hughes Brothers’ film “Menace II Society”, and for providing the voice of “Ryder” in the video game “Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas”.He performed the soundtrack to the movie Menace II Society called “Streight Up Menace” n he did it menacing;y At the moment, CMW is set for a reunion which will feature on [DJ Slip](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Slip)’s next album “The Minority Report”. MC Eiht also had a role in 1998’s movie, Thicker than Water as Lil Ant with [Mack 10](http://www.last.fm/music/Mack+10), [Fat Joe](http://www.last.fm/music/Fat+Joe), [Ice Cube](http://www.last.fm/music/Ice+Cube), [WC](http://www.last.fm/music/WC), CJ Mac, [Big Pun](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Big+Pun), and [Cypress Hill](http://www.last.fm/music/Cypress+Hill)’s [B-Real](http://www.last.fm/music/B-Real).      **MC Eiht solo albums:**   * Last Man Standing (Epic Street/Sony 1997)   * Section 8 (Hoo Bangin’/Priority 1999)   * N My Neighbourhood (2000)   * Tha8t’z Gangsta (Half-Ounce 2001)   * Underground Hero (D3 Entertainment 2002)   * Hood Arrest (Lookin Up Entertainment 2003)   * Veterans Day (West, Inc./Native Records 2005)   * Affiliated (Paid in Full Ent, 2006)   * Compton’s OG (Thump Records 2006)
