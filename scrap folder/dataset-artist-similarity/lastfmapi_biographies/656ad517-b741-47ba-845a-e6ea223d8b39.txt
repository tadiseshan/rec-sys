Maggie Reilly (born September 15, 1956) is a Scottish vocalist best known for her collaborations with the composer [Mike Oldfield](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Oldfield) between 1980 and 1984, especially by performing the vocals on such songs as “[Moonlight Shadow](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Oldfield/_/Moonlight+Shadow)”, “[Five Miles Out](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Oldfield/_/Five+Miles+Out)”, “[Family Man](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Oldfield/_/Family+Man)” and “[To France](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Oldfield/_/To+France)”.      Reilly was born in Glasgow. Her early career included singing with the Scottish Jazz Funk band [Cado Belle](http://www.last.fm/music/Cado+Belle). In 1992, she issued her debut solo album “[Echoes](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/Echoes)”, from which the singles “[Everytime We Touch](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/_/Everytime+We+Touch)”, “[Tears in the Rain](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/_/Tears+in+the+Rain)” and “[Wait](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/_/Wait)” were the most successful ones, followed by “[Midnight Sun](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/Midnight+Sun)” in 1993, “[Elena](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/Elena)” in 1996, “[Starcrossed](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/Starcrossed)” in 2000, “[Save It For A Rainy Day](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/Save+It+For+A+Rainy+Day)” in 2003 and “[ROWAN](http://www.last.fm/music/Maggie+Reilly/ROWAN)” in 2006.      She has also worked with many others including [Mike Batt](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Batt) (on his “[The Hunting Of The Snark](http://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Batt/The+Hunting+Of+The+Snark)” album), [Jack Bruce](http://www.last.fm/music/Jack+Bruce), [Dave Greenfield](http://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Greenfield) & Jan-Jacques Burnel, [Lesiëm](http://www.last.fm/music/Lesi%C3%ABm), [Ralph McTell](http://www.last.fm/music/Ralph+McTell), [Simon Nicol](http://www.last.fm/music/Simon+Nicol) (of [Fairport Convention](http://www.last.fm/music/Fairport+Convention)), [Runrig](http://www.last.fm/music/Runrig), [The Sisters of Mercy](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Sisters+of+Mercy) and [Smokie](http://www.last.fm/music/Smokie).
