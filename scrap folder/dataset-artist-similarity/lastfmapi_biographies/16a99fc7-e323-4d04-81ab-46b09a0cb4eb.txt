[Matt Redman](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman) is a British [christian](http://www.last.fm/tag/christian) [songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag/songwriter), and was one of the main [worship leader](http://www.last.fm/tag/worship%20leader)s associated with the [Soul Survivor](http://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Survivor) organization. He is now with Passion Conferences and an artist for Six Steps Records. He has authored and edited multiple books on Christian worship, including “The Unquenchable Worshipper” and the book [Facedown](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/Facedown) which accompanied the album. Some of his most popular songs include [Heart of Worship](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/_/Heart+of+Worship), [Let Everything That Has Breath](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/_/Let+Everyth ing+That+Has+Breath), [Once Again](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/_/Once+Again), and [Blessed Be Your Name](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/_/Blessed+Be+Your+Name), the latter winning the “Worship Song of the Year” award in the 2005 GMA music awards.      He has since launched another album, [Beautiful News](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/Beautiful+News), and continues to write many more popular songs such as [You Never Let Go](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/_/You+Never+Let+Go) and [Shine](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/_/Shine).      [Songfacts](http://www.songfacts.com/detail.php?id=23559) states that Redman’s eighth album [10,000 Reasons](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/10,000+Reasons), was co-written with several of his friends. He then recorded the songs live in Atlanta, leading worship for over 1,000 worship leaders during LIFT: A Worship Leader Collective, put on by Passion City Church and sixstepsrecords on February 18 and 19, 2011. It sold nearly 7,000 units its first week in the US, almost doubling Redman’s previous album release [We Shall Not Be Shaken](http://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Redman/We+Shall+Not+Be+Shaken). The LP entered the Billboard 200 at #66, his biggest debut to date.
