Johnny Flynn (also known as Joe Flynn, born 14 March 1983 in Cape Town) is a British [acoustic](http://www.last.fm/tag/acoustic) [singer- songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag/singer-songwriter) and actor. He has also performed and recorded with the English [folk-rock](http://www.last.fm/tag /folk-rock) act [Johnny Flynn & the Sussex Wit](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Johnny+Flynn+&+the+Sussex+Wit).      Flynn released his debut album, [A Larum](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn/A+Larum), in 2008. This was followed by the [sweet william ep](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn/sweet+william+ep) in 2009 and his second album [Been Listening](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn/Been+Listening) in 2010. ‘Been Listening’ includes the singles album [Kentucky Pill](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn/_/Kentucky+Pill), [Barnacled Warship](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn/_/Barnacled+Warship) and [The Water](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn/_/The+Water), which features a duet with [Laura Marling](http://www.last.fm/music/Laura+Marling).
