**Quintorigo** are an Italian band founded in 1996 by the ex-leader, ex singer [John De Leo](http://www.last.fm/music/John+De+Leo). Their genre vary from [experimental](http://www.last.fm/tag/experimental), [jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz), [pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/pop) and [rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/rock) music.   Although the musical ensemble is composed only by acoustic instruments, the band often make heavy use of sound effects like distortion, flanger, wah-wah to recreate most of the rock sound in their songs.   The current lineup is made by Luca Sapio (_voice_), Andrea Costa (_violin_), Gionata Costa (_cello_), Stefano Ricci (_upright bass_), Valentino Bianchi (_saxophone_).
