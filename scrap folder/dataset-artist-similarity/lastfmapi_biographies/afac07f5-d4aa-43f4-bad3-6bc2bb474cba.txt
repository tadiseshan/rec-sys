A trumpet player      1\. **Ray Davies** (born June 21, 1944 at Fortis Green, London, full name Raymond Douglas Davies) is an influential [english](http://www.last.fm/tag/english) [rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/rock) musician, best known as lead singer-songwriter for [The Kinks](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Kinks) \- one of the most influential, prolific and long-lived [british invasion](http://www.last.fm/tag/british%20invasion) bands - which he led with his younger brother, [Dave Davies](http://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Davies). He has also acted, directed and produced shows for theater and television.      Since the demise of The Kinks in the mid-90s Ray Davies has embarked on a critically successful solo career. His January 2006 release [Other People’s Lives](http://www.last.fm/music/Ray+Davies/Other+People%27s+Lives) was his first top 30 hit in the UK since 1980s, when he worked with The Kinks. He released his second solo release [Working Man’s Café](http://www.last.fm/music/Ray+Davies/Working+Man%27s+Caf%C3%A9) in October 2007.      2\. Ray Davies is a trumpeter and film composer.
