**Joseph Leslie “Joe” Sample** was an American pianist, keyboard player and composer. He was one of the founding members of the [Swingsters](http://www.last.fm/music/Swingsters), a band which evolved into [The Modern Jazz Sextet](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Modern+Jazz+Sextet) and later [The Jazz Crusaders](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Jazz+Crusaders). The band became simply [The Crusaders](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Crusaders) in 1971. He remained a part of the group until its final album in 1991.      Since the late 1980s, he has enjoyed a successful solo career.      Joe Sample died on September 12, 2014
