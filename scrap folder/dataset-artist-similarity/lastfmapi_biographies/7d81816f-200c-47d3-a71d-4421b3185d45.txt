Andy Summers is an English guitarist and composer, best known as the guitarist for [The Police](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Police).      Born in 1942, Andy started as a jazz guitarist. He was a member of Zoot Money, The Animals, and Soft Machine, but he became famous as the guitarist of The Police. In the 80’s he collaborated with [King Crimson](http://www.last.fm/music/King+Crimson) mastermind [Robert Fripp](http://www.last.fm/music/Robert+Fripp) and made some ambient/new age- oriented albums on his own as well. From the 90’s on he turned back to jazz and fusion.
