Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989 in Wyomissing, Pennsylvania) is an American [country pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/country%20pop) [singer- songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag/singer-songwriter) and actress.      In 2006, she released her debut single “[Tim McGraw](http://www.last.fm/music/Taylor+Swift/_/Tim+McGraw)”, then her [self- titled debut album](http://www.last.fm/music/taylor+swift/taylor+swift), which was subsequently certified multi-platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. In November 2008, Swift released her second album, _[Fearless](http://www.last.fm/music/Taylor+Swift/Fearless)_. _Fearless_ and _Taylor Swift_ finished 2008 at number-three and number-six respectively, with sales of 2.1 and 1.5 million. _Fearless_ has topped the _Billboard_ 200 in 11 non-consecutive weeks; no album has spent more time at No. 1 since 2000. Swift was named _Artist of the Year_ by Billboard Magazine in 2009. _Fearless_ won the Grammy Award for Album of the Year in 2010. Her third album, _Speak Now_ was released in November 2010.      In 2008, her albums sold a combined four million copies, making her the best- selling musician of the year in the United States, according to [Nielsen SoundScan](http://www.soundscan.com/). _Forbes_ ranked Swift 2009’s 69th-most powerful celebrity with earnings of $18 million. In January 2010 Nielsen SoundScan lists Swift as the top-selling digital artist in music history with over 24.3 million digital tracks sold. To date, she has sold over 20 million albums worldwide.
