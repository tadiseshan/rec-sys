Arve Henriksen (b. 1968) is a Norwegian trumpet player. His distinctive flute- like sound can be heard in such bands as [Supersilent](http://www.last.fm/music/Supersilent), [Christian Wallumrød Ensemble](http://www.last.fm/music/Christian+Wallumr%C3%B8d+Ensemble), [Trygve Seim](http://www.last.fm/music/Trygve+Seim) Ensemble, [Veslefrekk](http://www.last.fm/music/Veslefrekk), [Jon Balke & Magnetic North Orchestra](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Balke+&+Magnetic+North+Orchestra), and [Nine Horses](http://www.last.fm/music/Nine+Horses) among others.      Arve studied at University of Trondheim, Department of Music, and has since 1989 worked as a freelance musician. He has worked with a wide selection of musicians, like [Jon Balke](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Balke), [Anders Jormin](http://www.last.fm/music/Anders+Jormin), [Edward Vesala](http://www.last.fm/music/Edward+Vesala), [Jon Christensen](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Christensen), [Marilyn Mazur](http://www.last.fm/music/Marilyn+Mazur), [Marilyn Crispell](http://www.last.fm/music/Marilyn+Crispell), [Nils Petter Molvær](http://www.last.fm/music/Nils+Petter+Molv%C3%A6r), [Misha Alperin](http://www.last.fm/music/Misha+Alperin), [Arkady Shilkloper](http://www.last.fm/music/Arkady+Shilkloper), [Marc Ducret](http://www.last.fm/music/Marc+Ducret), [Ketil Bjørnstad](http://www.last.fm/music/Ketil+Bj%C3%B8rnstad), [David Sylvian](http://www.last.fm/music/David+Sylvian), [Steve Jansen](http://www.last.fm/music/Steve+Jansen) and [Tore Brunborg](http://www.last.fm/music/Tore+Brunborg).
