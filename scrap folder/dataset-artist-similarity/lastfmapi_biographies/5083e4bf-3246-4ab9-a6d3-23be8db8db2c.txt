Olivia Ruiz (born Olivia Blanc) is a [female](http://www.last.fm/tag/female) [french](http://www.last.fm/tag/french) singer born on January 1, 1980. Olivia participated on the French TV show “Star Academy”, which exposed her not only to the public but also to the music industry which was soon convinced of her special talent.      Since then, Olivia has embarked on her own career and has worked on duets with a number of artists: [Weepers Circus](http://www.last.fm/music/Weepers+Circus), Christian Olivier from [Les Têtes Raides](http://www.last.fm/music/Les+T%C3%AAtes+Raides); she has worked with noted French song-writers: [Ben Ricour](http://www.last.fm/music/Ben+Ricour), Christophe Mali from [Tryo](http://www.last.fm/music/Tryo) and Mathias Malzieu leader of [Dionysos](http://www.last.fm/music/Dionysos).      Her latest album is “Miss Météores”
