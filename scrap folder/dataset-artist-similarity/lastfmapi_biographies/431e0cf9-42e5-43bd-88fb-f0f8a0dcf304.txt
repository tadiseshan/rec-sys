Embee is a prominent Swedish hip hop DJ and producer affilliated to [Soundism](http://www.soundism.com/en/music-producers/embee.htm) . He was born Magnus Bergqvist in Sweden in the late 1970s. He is best known for his work with Swedish hip-hop group [Looptroop](http://www.last.fm/music/Looptroop). His first solo album “Tellings from Solitaria” won a Swedish Grammy Award for best hip hop or soul release of 2004.
