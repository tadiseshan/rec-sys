Inara George (born July 4, 1974) is a Los Angeles, California-based singer- songwriter, one half of [The Bird and the Bee](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Bird+and+the+Bee) and a member of the band [Merrick](http://www.last.fm/music/Merrick), with [Bryony Atkinson](http://www.last.fm/music/Bryony+Atkinson). Her 2006 solo album All Rise was a KCRW hit. George is the daughter of [Lowell George](http://www.last.fm/music/Lowell+George), the late founder of the rock and roll group [Little Feat](http://www.last.fm/music/Little+Feat). She was five years old when he passed away. Inara is married to director Jake Kasdan.      George has appeared on two of [Idlewild](http://www.last.fm/music/Idlewild)’s studio albums, providing backing vocals, and has recorded with the jazz/rock group [Kneebody](http://www.last.fm/music/Kneebody) (formerly known as Wendel), on their first release; she sang the introduction to the song “I’ll Watch Your Life to See”.      In 2006, George recorded an album with [Idlewild](http://www.last.fm/music/Idlewild) guitarist, [Rod Jones](http://www.last.fm/music/Rod+Jones), entitled [George is Jones](http://www.last.fm/music/George+is+Jones). The album remains unreleased, however, four songs can be heard on the duo’s MySpace.      In August 2008 second studio album An Invitation was released.
