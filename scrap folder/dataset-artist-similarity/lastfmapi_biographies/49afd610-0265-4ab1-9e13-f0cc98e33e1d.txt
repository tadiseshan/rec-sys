**Jan Hammer** (born 17 April 1948, in then Czechoslovakia, today Prague, Czech Republic) is a polyinstrumentalist, composer, pianist and keyboardist.      Because of the Soviet invasion, in 1968 he went to the USA, where he started his musical career with the legendary [Mahavishnu Orchestra](http://www.last.fm/music/Mahavishnu+Orchestra). He worked with many different musicians, including Jeff Beck, Al DiMeola, Eric Clapton, Jerry Goodman, Mick Jagger and others. He also led his own [Jan Hammer Group](http://www.last.fm/music/Jan+Hammer+Group) from 1976 till 1980. As a player, he is quickly recognizable for his excellent guitar-like solos. Jan Hammer is a famous music composer for many TV shows between the 1970s and 90s. The most famous one is [Miami Vice](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0086759/), he wrote the music for 90 episodes (4 first seasons) and he’s the creator of the worldwide #1 hit theme song : [Crockett’s Theme](http://www.last.fm/music/Jan+Hammer/_/Crockett%27s+Theme).
