Hailing from Durham-Raleigh, North Carolina, Edgar Allen Floe has made a name for himself, writing and producing as one of 15 members in the [Justus League](http://www.last.fm/music/Justus+League). Other members include [Khrysis](http://www.last.fm/music/Khrysis) and [9th Wonder](http://www.last.fm/music/9th+Wonder). He produces under the name Slicemysta and is a member of [The Undefined](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Undefined).      He has released one EP, 2005’s [True Links](http://www.last.fm/music/Edgar+Allen+Floe/True+Links), and three mixtapes, 2006’s [Floe Almighty: The Chronicles Of Edgar Allen Floe](http://ww w.last.fm/music/Edgar+Allen+Floe/Floe+Almighty:+The+Chronicles+Of+Edgar+Allen+ Floe), 2007’s remixed version, [Floe Almighty: The Remixture](http://www.last. fm/music/Edgar+Allen+Floe/Floe+Almighty:+The+Remixture), and 2008’s [The Road To Streetwise](http://www.last.fm/music/Edgar+Allen+Floe/The+Road+To+Streetwise). All contain production from Khrysis and 9th Wonder.      Floe released his first full-length album, [The Streetwise LP](http://www.last.fm/music/Edgar+Allen+Floe/The+Streetwise+LP), on October 28, 2008, on his own record label, MCEO Records, just as True Links was. The Streetwise LP features guest appearances from [Median](http://www.last.fm/music/Median), [L.E.G.A.C.Y.](http://www.last.fm/music/L.E.G.A.C.Y.) and [Sean Boog](http://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Boog) of [The Away Team](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Away+Team), and was posititively received by critics, with one stating “Floe raps from a pulpit of authority that not many emcees rival”.      He is also planning to release an album with [Mal Demolish](http://www.last.fm/music/Mal+Demolish). It will be the first official work released by his group, The Undefined.
