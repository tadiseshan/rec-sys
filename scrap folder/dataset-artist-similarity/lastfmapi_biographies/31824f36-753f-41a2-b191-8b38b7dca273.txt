Pigeon John is a hip hop artist from Los Angeles. He entered the hip hop game via the famous open mic nights at the Good Life Cafe during the 90s. He is one half of the hip hop groups [Brainwash Projects](http://www.last.fm/music/Brainwash+Projects) and [Rootbeer](http://www.last.fm/music/Rootbeer) and is a former member of the hip hop crew [L.A. Symphony](http://www.last.fm/music/L.A.+Symphony). He is currently signed to Quannum Records, the family home of artists such as [Blackalicious](http://www.last.fm/music/Blackalicious), [Lyrics Born](http://www.last.fm/music/Lyrics+Born), [Lateef The Truth Speaker](http://www.last.fm/music/Lateef+The+Truth+Speaker) and [DJ Shadow](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Shadow).
