[Tim Hughes](http://www.last.fm/music/Tim+Hughes) is a British-based Christian music artist, worship leader, and songwriter. Widely respected in Christian music circles, Tim is renowned for his Dove Award-winning modern worship anthem: [Here I Am to Worship](http://www.last.fm/music/Tim+Hughes/_/Here+I+Am+to+Worship).      Tim & his wife Rachel have been involved with [Soul Survivor](http://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Survivor) events around the world as well as the [Soul Survivor Watford](http://www.soulsurvivorwatford.co.uk/) church for many years.      Tim now heads up the worship at [Holy Trinity Brompton](http://www.htb.org.uk/), having previously been Worship Pastor at Soul Survivor Watford (a post now held by [Beth Croft](http://www.last.fm/music/Beth+Croft) nee [Beth Coulson](http://www.last.fm/music/Beth+Coulson)) and is passionate about resourcing musicians and worship leaders.      <http://www.worshipcentral.org/blog/tim/>.
