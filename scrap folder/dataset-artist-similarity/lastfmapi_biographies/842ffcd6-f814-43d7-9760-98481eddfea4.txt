Nichole Nordeman is an award-winning contemporary Christian singer-songwriter from the United States.      Nichole Nordeman was raised in Colorado Springs, Colorado, where she played the piano in church. She got her start when she entered a Gospel Music Association (GMA) contest in Los Angeles, California and was noticed by a music producer. She has won multiple GMA Dove Awards, including two awards for Female Vocalist of the Year. Her best-known songs include “Why,” “This Mystery,” “Holy”, “Legacy,” “Brave,” and “What If.” She also sang a song on the soundtrack Music Inspired by the Chronicles of Narnia, called “I Will Believe”
