Derek Sherinian is a keyboardist based in Los Angeles, California. His most notable achievements are as a member of the bands [Alice Cooper](http://www.last.fm/music/Alice+Cooper), [Kiss](http://www.last.fm/music/Kiss) and [Dream Theater](http://www.last.fm/music/Dream+Theater). Sherinian has been called the “King of the Keys” and the “Caligula of keyboards” due to his performing style. His influences include [Jan Hammer](http://www.last.fm/music/Jan+Hammer) ([Mahavishnu Orchestra](http://www.last.fm/music/Mahavishnu+Orchestra)), [Keith Emerson](http://www.last.fm/music/Keith+Emerson) ([Emerson, Lake & Palmer](http://www.last.fm/music/Emerson,+Lake+&+Palmer) and [The Nice](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Nice)), [Rick Wakeman](http://www.last.fm/music/Rick+Wakeman) ([Yes](http://www.last.fm/music/Yes)), [Elton John](http://www.last.fm/music/Elton+John), and a variety of guitarists. Sherinian endorses various equipment, including Korg and Clavia keyboards. He is of Armenian and Greek descent.      With [Dream Theater](http://www.last.fm/music/Dream+Theater), he recorded the albums [A Change of Seasons](http://www.last.fm/music/Dream+Theater/A+Change+of+Seasons) (1995), [Falling Into Infinity](http://www.last.fm/music/Dream+Theater/Falling+Into+Infinity) (1997) and [Once in a Livetime](http://www.last.fm/music/Dream+Theater/Once+in+a+Livetime) (1998) before being replaced in 1999 with Jordan Rudess due to creative differences.      Since 2000, Sherinian has recorded several solo records on which artists like [Yngwie Malmsteen](http://www.last.fm/music/Yngwie+Malmsteen), [Zakk Wylde](http://www.last.fm/music/Zakk+Wylde) and [Steve Lukather](http://www.last.fm/music/Steve+Lukather) have lent their talents. Most notable of all [Planet X](http://www.last.fm/music/Planet+X), recorded with Australian drummer [Virgil Donati](http://www.last.fm/music/Virgil+Donati), which led to the formation of the group of the same name, as Sherinian and Donati decided to expand on the concept by recruiting guitar virtuoso [Tony MacAlpine](http://www.last.fm/music/Tony+MacAlpine). Since 2002, Sherinian has been regularly touring with [Billy Idol](http://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Idol) as well as recording several tribute records. Sherinian’s fifth solo album, [Blood Of The Snake](http://www.last.fm/music/Derek+Sherinian/Blood+Of+The+Snake) (2006), features [Zakk Wylde](http://www.last.fm/music/Zakk+Wylde), [John Petrucci](http://www.last.fm/music/John+Petrucci), [Yngwie Malmsteen](http://www.last.fm/music/Yngwie+Malmsteen), [Slash](http://www.last.fm/music/Slash), [Billy Idol](http://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Idol), Steve Lukather, Simon Phillips, Tony Franklin, Brad Gillis, and Brian Tichy.
