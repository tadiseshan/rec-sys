SafetySuit is an alternative rock band from Tulsa, Oklahoma currently based in Nashville, Tennessee. According to the [Believe Songfacts](http://www.songfacts.com/detail.php?id=25067), singer [Douglas Brown](http://www.last.fm/music/Douglas+Brown), drummer [Tate Cunningham](http://www.last.fm/music/Tate+Cunningham), bassist Jeremy Henshaw and guitarist [Dave Garofalo](http://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Garofalo) met while attending Oral Roberts University.      SafetySuit are notable for their song [Someone Like You](http://www.last.fm/music/SafetySuit/_/Someone+Like+You) off of their 2008 debut major label album [Life Left To Go](http://www.last.fm/music/SafetySuit/Life+Left+To+Go), and [Anywhere But Here](http://www.last.fm/music/SafetySuit/_/Anywhere+But+Here) that was played in Kyle XY. [Someone Like You](http://www.last.fm/music/SafetySuit/_/Someone+Like+You) peaked at #15 on the Alternative rock chart and has crossed over to Top 40 and Hot AC. Their single [Stay](http://www.last.fm/music/SafetySuit/_/Stay) topped at #1 on VH1’s Top 20 Countdown. The song has achieved great success, and the video is a top seller on itunes. Lead singer Doug Brown actually got hit with an eagle statue by the woman in the video, and has a scar by his eye to prove it! Recently, they have toured with 3 Doors Down and Seether, and their current tour schedule includes performances with Collective Soul and Ryan Star.
