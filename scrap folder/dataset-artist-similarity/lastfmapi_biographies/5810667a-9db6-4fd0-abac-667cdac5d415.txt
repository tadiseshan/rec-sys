**Chester Burton Atkins** born 20 June 1924 - Died 30 June 2001 in Nashville, Tennessee, United States (1942–1996) . was an American guitarist and record producer. Together with [Owen Bradley](http://www.last.fm/music/Owen+Bradley), Atkins created the smoother [country](http://www.last.fm/tag/country) music style known as the Nashville sound, which expanded country music’s appeal to include adult pop music fans as well. His virtuoso picking style - inspired by [Merle Travis](http://www.last.fm/music/Merle+Travis), [Django Reinhardt](http://www.last.fm/music/Django+Reinhardt), [George Barnes](http://www.last.fm/music/George+Barnes) and [Les Paul](http://www.last.fm/music/Les+Paul) \- brought him admirers both within and outside the country scene. Atkins produced records for [Eddy Arnold](http://www.last.fm/music/Eddy+Arnold), [Don Gibson](http://www.last.fm/music/Don+Gibson), [Jim Reeves](http://www.last.fm/music/Jim+Reeves), [Jerry Reed](http://www.last.fm/music/Jerry+Reed), [Connie Smith](http://www.last.fm/music/Connie+Smith), and [Waylon Jennings](http://www.last.fm/music/Waylon+Jennings).
