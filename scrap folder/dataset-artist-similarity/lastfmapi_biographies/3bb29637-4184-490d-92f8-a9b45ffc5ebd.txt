Jonathan Mark Foreman (born October 22, 1976) also known as [Jonathan Foreman](http://www.last.fm/music/Jonathan+Foreman) is the lead singer, songwriter and guitarist of the [alternative rock](http://www.last.fm/tag/alternative%20rock) band [Switchfoot](http://www.last.fm/music/Switchfoot); one part of the alt. folk duo [Fiction Family](http://www.last.fm/music/Fiction+Family) with [Sean Watkins](http://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Watkins) from [Nickel Creek](http://www.last.fm/music/Nickel+Creek); and a [folk](http://www.last.fm/tag/folk)-[singer-songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag /singer-songwriter) solo artist.      Jon Foreman has released four conceptual EPs as a solo artist: “[Fall](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Foreman/Fall)” & “[Winter](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Foreman/Winter)” (2007) “[Spring](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Foreman/Spring)” & “[Summer](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Foreman/Summer)” (2008). He later compiled several songs from each EP into the LP “[Limbs And Branches](http://www.last.fm/music/Jon+Foreman/Limbs+And+Branches)” (2008), which included two new songs.      In 2001 he was awarded the “Les Paul Horizon Award” for the most promising up- and-coming guitarist at the annual Orville H. Gibson Guitar Awards in Los Angeles.
