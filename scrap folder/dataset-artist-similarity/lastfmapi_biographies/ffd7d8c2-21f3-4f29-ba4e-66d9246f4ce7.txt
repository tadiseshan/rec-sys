Greg Edmonson is a music composer for television and movies. He is primarily known for composing the soundtrack of the Uncharted video games and the cancelled TV series Firefly.      Greg Edmonson grew up in Dallas, Texas and played the guitar as a youth. He studied jazz composition at North Texas State University. Later, while he was a studio musician and session player, he went to the Musicians Institute of Technology. He has studied with Dr. Albert Harris, the former musical director for NBC and a leading composition teacher and music lecturer.
