Per Håkan Gessle (born January 12, 1959) is the songwriter and male lead singer of the Swedish bands [Gyllene Tider](http://www.last.fm/music/Gyllene+Tider) and [Roxette](http://www.last.fm/music/Roxette). He was born in Halmstad, Sweden. He was a founding member of Gyllene Tider in 1979, and together with a friend, singer [Marie Fredriksson](http://www.last.fm/music/Marie+Fredriksson), started the successful band Roxette in 1986.      He has also participated with the Swedish singer [Nisse Hellberg](http://www.last.fm/music/Nisse+Hellberg) in the project [The Lonely Boys](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Lonely+Boys).      As of 2012, he has released seven solo albums:   * Per Gessle (1983)   * Scener (1985)   * The World According To Gessle (1997)   * Mazarin (2003)   * Son of a Plumber (2005)   * En händig man (2007)   * Party Crasher (2008)
