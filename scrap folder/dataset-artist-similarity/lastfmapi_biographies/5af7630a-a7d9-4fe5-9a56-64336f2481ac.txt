Francesco Renga (born in Udine, June 12, 1968) is an Italian singer- songwriter. He won the Sanremo Music Festival in 2005 with the song Angelo.      He is romantically involved with actress Ambra Angiolini, who has borne him two children, Jolanda and Leonardo.
