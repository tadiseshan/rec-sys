Idris Muhammad (born November 13, 1939 in New Orleans, Louisiana) is a [jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz) [drummer](http://www.last.fm/tag/drummer). He was born Leo Morris on November 13, 1939 before changing his name in the 1960s upon his conversion to Islam. He is known for his [funky](http://www.last.fm/tag/funky) playing style. He has released a number of albums as leader, and has played with a number of [jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz) legends including [Lou Donaldson](http://www.last.fm/music/Lou+Donaldson), [Johnny Griffin](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Griffin), [Pharoah Sanders](http://www.last.fm/music/Pharoah+Sanders) and Grover Washington, Jr. He currently tours internationally with [pianist](http://www.last.fm/tag/pianist) [Ahmad Jamal](http://www.last.fm/music/Ahmad+Jamal).      In 1966, he married Dolores “LaLa” Brooks (former member of the Crystals; she converted to Islam with him and went for a time under the name Sakinah Muhammad). They separated in 1999. Together, they have two sons and two daughters.
