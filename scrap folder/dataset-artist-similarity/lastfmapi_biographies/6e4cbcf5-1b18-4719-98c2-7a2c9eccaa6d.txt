**Manley Augustus Buchanan** (born April 19, 1949, Trenchtown, Kingston, [Jamaica](http://www.last.fm/place/Jamaica)), better known as [Big Youth](http://www.last.fm/music/Big+Youth) (sometimes called [Jah Youth](http://www.last.fm/music/Jah+Youth)), is a Jamaican DJ, mostly known for his work during the 1970s. Influenced by [U-Roy](http://www.last.fm/music/U-Roy), he started singing with Lord Tippertone’s sound system in 1970. His first LP Chi Chi Run was produced by [Prince Buster](http://www.last.fm/music/Prince+Buster) in 1971.      The name of the band [Sonic Youth](http://www.last.fm/music/Sonic+Youth) is in part a tribute to [Big Youth](http://www.last.fm/music/Big+Youth).      “Yes, me come inna de music as Rasta, me a de original rastaman who enter it.” [Big Youth](http://www.last.fm/music/Big+Youth), [Italy](http://www.last.fm/place/Italy), 2001      Discography      * Chi Chi Run - Fab 1972 (including just three Big Youth songs)   * Screaming Target - Trojan 1973   * Reggae Phenomenon - Augustus Buchanen 1975   * Dreadlocks Dread - Klick 1975   * Natty Cultural Dread - Trojan 1976   * Hit the Road Jack - Trojan 1976   * Isiah First Prohphet of Old - Nicola Delita, Caroline 1978   * Progress - Nocola Delita 1979   * Rock Holy - Negusa Negast 1980   * Some Great Big Youth - Heartbeat 1981   * Chanting Dread Inna Fine Style - Heartbeat 1982   * Live at Reggae Sunsplash - Genes 1984   * A Luta Continua (The Struggle Continue) - Heartbeat 1986   * Manifestation - Heartbeat 1988   * Jamming in the House of Dread - Danceteria 1990   * Higher Grounds - JR, VP Records 1995   * Save the children - Declic 1995   * Reggae Gi Dem Dub - Nicola Delita
