Behind the name **The Tallest Man On Earth** is [Kristian Matsson](http://www.last.fm/music/Kristian+Matsson) (born April 30th, 1983 in Dalarna, Sweden). He plays guitar, banjo and piano and has released three full length albums, the first entitled [Shallow Grave](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Tallest+Man+on+Earth/Shallow+Grave) and the second entitled [The Wild Hunt](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Tallest+Man+on+Earth/The+Wild+Hunt). The Tallest Man On Earth is known for his very charismatic raw voice. He is often compared to [Bob Dylan](http://www.last.fm/music/Bob+Dylan), who shared his raw vocal style and folk-based musical style.      He was formerly the front man in [Montezumas](http://www.last.fm/music/Montezumas).
