Kris Delmhorst (reputedly born in 1970 in Brooklyn) is an American [folk](http://www.last.fm/tag/folk) singer based in Boston. In 1998, after several years of experience of performing live, Delmhorst finally finished recording her debut album, [Appetite](http://www.last.fm/music/Kris+Delmhorst/Appetite). In the same year she was involved in producing the Respond compilation, a fundraiser for domestic violence groups, which included her song “Weatherman”.      In 1999, Delmhorst released a live album with [the vinal avenue string band](http://www.last.fm/music/the+vinal+avenue+string+band), consisting of herself, [Sean Staples](http://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Staples), and [Ry Cavanaugh](http://www.last.fm/music/Ry+Cavanaugh).      Delmhorst is married to [Jeffrey Foucault](http://www.last.fm/music/Jeffrey+Foucault). In 2003, Delmhorst, Foucault, and Peter Mulvey released an album entitled [Redbird](http://www.last.fm/music/Redbird/Redbird).      Delmhorst’s more recent albums include [Strange Conversation](http://www.last.fm/music/Kris+Delmhorst/Strange+Conversation) (2006), [Shotgun singer](http://www.last.fm/music/Kris+Delmhorst/Shotgun+singer) (2008), and [Cars](http://www.last.fm/music/Kris+Delmhorst/Cars) (2011).
