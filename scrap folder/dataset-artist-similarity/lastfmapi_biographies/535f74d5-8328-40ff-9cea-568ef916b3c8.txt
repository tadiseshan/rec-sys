**The D.O.C.** (born Tracy Lynn Curry June 10, 1968) is an American rapper from Dallas, Texas who moved to Compton, California, to join the creative force behind the rap group [N.W.A](http://www.last.fm/music/N.W.A) Prior to his move, he was a member of the [the fila fresh crew](http://www.last.fm/music/the+fila+fresh+crew).      The D.O.C released his first solo album (produced by Dr. Dre) in 1989 called ’ [No One Can Do It Better](http://www.last.fm/music/The+D.O.C./No+One+Can+Do+It+Better) ‘. This album is widely heralded as a classic. He was involved in a car crash in the early 90’s that left him with a crushed larynx which altered his vocal timbre. He has since released albums ‘[Helter Skelter](http://www.last.fm/music/The+D.O.C./Helter+Skelter)’ and ‘[Deuce](http://www.last.fm/music/The+D.O.C./Deuce)’. He has continued to be a major influence on rap music by ghostwriting on Dr. Dre’s projects, including classic albums such as ‘[The Chronic](http://www.last.fm/music/Dr.+Dre/The+Chronic)’ and ‘Doggystyle’.
