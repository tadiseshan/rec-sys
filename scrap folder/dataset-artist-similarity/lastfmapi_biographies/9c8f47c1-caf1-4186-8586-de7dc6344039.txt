**Vast Aire** (b. February 5, 1978) is a [rapper](http://www.last.fm/tag/rapper) from New York City. He is half of the New York [hip-hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip-hop) duo [Cannibal Ox](http://www.last.fm/music/Cannibal+Ox), which consists of himself and fellow rapper [Vordul Mega](http://www.last.fm/music/Vordul+Mega). He is also a member of the rap group [Atoms Family](http://www.last.fm/music/Atoms+Family). He was born in Mount Vernon, New York, then lived in Jamaica, Queens before moving to Harlem, where he became acquainted with the [underground hip-hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/underground%20hip-hop) scene, performing in many clubs while still a teenager.       Vast Aire’s fame increased significantly as part of Cannibal Ox. Together with record [producer](http://www.last.fm/tag/producer) [El-P](http://www.last.fm/music/El-P), their 2001 debut titled [The Cold Vein](http://www.last.fm/music/Cannibal+Ox/The+Cold+Vein) that was a critical success. Since then, Vast Aire has pursued solo interests. His first solo album “Look Mom… No Hands” was released in April 2004. This was followed by “The Best Damn Rap Show” with [DJ Mighty Mi](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Mighty+Mi) from [The High And Mighty](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/The+High+And+Mighty) & [Smut Peddlers](http://www.last.fm/music/Smut+Peddlers) in 2005. And collaboration project called [Mighty Joseph](http://www.last.fm/music/Mighty+Joseph) with [Karniege](http://www.last.fm/music/Karniege) in 2007. In 2008 Vast was released his second solo-LP, “Dueces Wild”. And “OX 2010 A Street Odyssey” in 2011.      In addition to El-P, legendary producers [Pete Rock](http://www.last.fm/music/Pete+Rock) and [The RZA](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/The+RZA) have reportedly signed on to contribute to the project.
