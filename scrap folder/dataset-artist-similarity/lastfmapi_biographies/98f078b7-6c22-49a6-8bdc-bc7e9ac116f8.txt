**Ricky Dunigan** (November 17, 1973 - December 20, 2013), better known by his stage name **Lord Infamous**, was an American [hip hop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hip%20hop) artist from Memphis, Tennessee, USA. He was co-founder of the Memphis rap group [Three 6 Mafia](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia), together with his half brother [DJ Paul](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Paul).      Lord Infamous began his music career in 1991 with his younger half-brother Paul Beauregard, better known by his stage name [DJ Paul](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Paul). Later in 1993, Lord Infamous and [DJ Paul](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Paul) met up with fellow north Memphis rapper and producer [Juicy J](http://www.last.fm/music/Juicy+J). These three along with a number of other local rappers formed a group initially called “The Backyard Posse”, which later went on the be known as “[Triple 6 Mafia](http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Triple+6+Mafia)”. The first record deal for the group was with Nick Scarfo and Prophet Posse. Under the label they released their first underground album, _[Smoked Out, Loced Out](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia/Smoked+Out,+Loced+Out)_ in 1994. At the same time, Lord Infamous released his first underground solo album, _[Lord Of Terror](http://www.last.fm/music/Lord+Infamous/Lord+Of+Terror)_. Shortly after in 1995, the group changed its name to [Three 6 Mafia](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia) and released their debut album, _[Mystic Stylez](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia/Mystic+Stylez)_. At this time [Three 6 Mafia](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia) members [DJ Paul](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Paul) and [Juicy J](http://www.last.fm/music/Juicy+J) were creating their own record company, [Hypnotize Minds](http://www.last.fm/label/Hypnotize+Minds), which Lord Infamous became a part of. The group went on to release 4 more mainstream albums and numerous solo projects through the mid-2000s, though during this time the group became smaller due to the departure of several artists for various reasons. In 2006, following jail time which lead to a breach of contract with Sony, Lord Infamous was forced to end work with [Three 6 Mafia](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia).      Lord Infamous played a critical role in the underground success of the multi platinum-selling group. Best known for his ability to rap in many different styles, and his lyrics touching on occult subject matters such as satanism, mass murder, suicide, and torture, he was considered the darker member of the group.      Following his departure from [Three 6 Mafia](http://www.last.fm/music/Three+6+Mafia) in 2006, Lord Infamous together with long time friend and Memphis rapper II Tone partnered to create a new record company, “Black Rain Entertainment”.
