**Lizz Wright** is a U.S. jazz singer and composer.      In 2000 Wright joined the Atlanta-based vocal quartet [In The Spirit](http://www.last.fm/music/In+The+Spirit), which soon achieved critical acclaim; in 2002 she signed a recording contract with Verve Records. Her first album, _[Salt](http://www.last.fm/music/Lizz+Wright/Salt)_, was released in 2003 (reaching number two on the Billboard Top Contemporary Jazz chart in 2004), followed by _[Dreaming Wide Awake](http://www.last.fm/music/Lizz+Wright/Dreaming+Wide+Awake)_ in June 2005 (reaching number one on the Top Contemporary Jazz chart in 2005 and 2006).
