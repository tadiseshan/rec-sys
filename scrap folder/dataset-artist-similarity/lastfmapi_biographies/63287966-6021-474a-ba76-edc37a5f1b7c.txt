Jeff Tweedy (born August 25, 1967 in Belleville, Illinois, United States) is an American singer-songwriter, musician, and poet best known for his work with the group [Wilco](http://www.last.fm/music/Wilco). Tweedy joined rockabilly band [The Plebes](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Plebes) with high school friend [Jay Farrar](http://www.last.fm/music/Jay+Farrar) in the early 1980s, but Tweedy’s musical interests caused one of Farrar’s brothers to quit. The Plebes changed their name to [The Primitives](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Primitives) in 1984, and subsequently to [Uncle Tupelo](http://www.last.fm/music/Uncle+Tupelo). Jeff is also a member of [Golden Smog](http://www.last.fm/music/Golden+Smog), an occasional musical collective whose shifting personnel also includes members of [The Jayhawks](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Jayhawks), [Soul Asylum](http://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Asylum) and [The Replacements](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Replacements). Jeff is also a member of [Loose Fur](http://www.last.fm/music/Loose+Fur), which includes [Jim O’Rourke](http://www.last.fm/music/Jim+O%27Rourke) ([Sonic Youth](http://www.last.fm/music/Sonic+Youth)) and [Glenn Kotche](http://www.last.fm/music/Glenn+Kotche) ([Wilco](http://www.last.fm/music/Wilco)). Further, as part of ([Wilco](http://www.last.fm/music/Wilco)) Tweedy teamed up with [Billy Bragg](http://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Bragg) to set Guthrie family-held lyrics of [Woody Guthrie](http://www.last.fm/music/Woody+Guthrie)’s to new music on the critically acclaimed albums [Mermaid Avenue](http://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Bragg+&+Wilco/Mermaid+Avenue) and [Mermaid Avenue Vol II](http://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Bragg+&+Wilco/Mermaid+Avenue+Vol+II).
