Gary Bartz (born in 1940) is an American alto and soprano saxophonist.      Since graduating from The Juilliard School he has worked with [Charles Mingus](http://www.last.fm/music/Charles+Mingus), [Art Blakey & The Jazz Messengers](http://www.last.fm/music/Art+Blakey+&+The+Jazz+Messengers), [Miles Davis](http://www.last.fm/music/Miles+Davis), [McCoy Tyner](http://www.last.fm/music/McCoy+Tyner), [Max Roach](http://www.last.fm/music/Max+Roach), and [Jackie McLean](http://www.last.fm/music/Jackie+McLean), as well as performing and recording with his own groups, among them the [Gary Bartz Ntu Troop](http://www.last.fm/music/Gary+Bartz+Ntu+Troop), which combined [soul](http://www.last.fm/tag/soul), [funk](http://www.last.fm/tag/funk), [african folk music](http://www.last.fm/tag/african%20folk%20music), [hard bop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hard%20bop), and [avant-garde jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/avant-garde%20jazz).
