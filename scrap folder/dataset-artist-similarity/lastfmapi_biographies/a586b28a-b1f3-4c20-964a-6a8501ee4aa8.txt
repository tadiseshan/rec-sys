Iain Archer is a singer/songwriter from [northern ireland](http://www.last.fm/tag/northern%20ireland), who was once a member of the indie band [Snow Patrol](http://www.last.fm/music/Snow+Patrol). Iain comes from a musical background and released several solo albums in the mid 90s on the small Scottish independent label Sticky Music. After a stint with Snow Patrol and a side project with Snow Patrol’s frontman [Gary Lightbody](http://www.last.fm/music/Gary+Lightbody) in [The Reindeer Section](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Reindeer+Section), he returned to his solo career. In 2004 he released his album [Flood the Tanks](http://www.last.fm/music/Iain+Archer/Flood+the+Tanks).      In 2005 Iain picked up an Ivor Novello Award as part of the songwriting team behind Snow Patrol’s [Final Straw](http://www.last.fm/music/Snow+Patrol/Final+Straw) album.      In 2006 he released the album [Magnetic North](http://www.last.fm/music/Iain+Archer/Magnetic+North)
