**Dot Allison** (born Dorothy Elliot Allison on 17 August 1969 in Edinburgh, Scotland) is a singer/songwriter who has made significant inroads in electronic music circles, most notably as a result of her tenure fronting the band [One Dove](http://www.last.fm/music/One+Dove) from 1991 to 1995.      Following the split of [One Dove](http://www.last.fm/music/One+Dove) in 1995, she released her debut single [Tomorrow Never Comes](http://www.last.fm/music/Dot+Allison/_/Tomorrow+Never+Comes) in 1998 and the following year saw the LP, _[Afterglow](http://www.last.fm/music/Dot+Allison/Afterglow)_, released to generally positive reviews. A song-oriented pop outing, it was followed by the electro and house music inspired _[We Are Science](http://www.last.fm/music/Dot+Allison/We+Are+Science)_ in 2002. Following her [folk](http://www.last.fm/tag/folk)-like third effort, _[Exaltation Of Larks](http://www.last.fm/music/Dot+Allison/Exaltation+Of+Larks)_, she continued further down this path and released her fourth solo album _[Room 7 1/2](http://www.last.fm/music/Dot+Allison/Room+7+1%2F2)_ in August 2009.      In addition to solo ventures, Allison’s career has concentrated on collaborative efforts, and has seen her work beside the likes of [Death in Vegas](http://www.last.fm/music/Death+in+Vegas), [Hal David](http://www.last.fm/music/Hal+David), [Arab Strap](http://www.last.fm/music/Arab+Strap), Scottish dance troupe [Slam](http://www.last.fm/music/Slam), [Kevin Shields](http://www.last.fm/music/Kevin+Shields) (of [My Bloody Valentine](http://www.last.fm/music/My+Bloody+Valentine)), [Peter Doherty](htt p://www.last.fm/music/Peter+Doherty)/[Babyshambles](http://www.last.fm/music/B abyshambles), Gary ‘Mani’ Mounfield (of [The Stone Roses](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Stone+Roses) & [Primal Scream](http://www.last.fm/music/Primal+Scream)) and club stalwarts [Massive Attack](http://www.last.fm/music/Massive+Attack). Dot recently dueted on the co-write Sheepskin Tearaway on [Peter Doherty](http://www.last.fm/music/Peter+Doherty)’s solo album.      Allison’s touring group includes members of [The Fall](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Fall), [Two Lone Swordsmen](http://www.last.fm/music/Two+Lone+Swordsmen), [Tindersticks](http://www.last.fm/music/Tindersticks), [Death in Vegas](http://www.last.fm/music/Death+in+Vegas), [Massive Attack](http://www.last.fm/music/Massive+Attack), and [Tokyo Windbag](http://www.last.fm/music/Tokyo+Windbag).
