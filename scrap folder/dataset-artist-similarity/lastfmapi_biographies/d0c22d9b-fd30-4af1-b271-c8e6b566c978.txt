**Louis Sclavis** (b. Lyon, France, February 2, 1953) is a French musician. He performs on clarinet, bass clarinet, and saxophones in a variety of contexts, including [jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz) and [free jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/free%20jazz). His music shows great creativity and a lively sense of humor. He frequently performs with the cellist Ernst Reijseger and was one of the first to combine jazz with French [folk](http://www.last.fm/tag/folk) music, working most prominently with the hurdy-gurdy player Valentin Clastrier. He is also famous with the trio involving French bassist Henri Texier and drummer Aldo Romano.
