Bitty McLean is a reggae music singer, producer and musician.      His career took off in 1994 with covers of [The Shirelles](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Shirelles)’ “Dedicated To The One I Love” and [Fats Domino](http://www.last.fm/music/Fats+Domino)’s “It Keeps Rainin (Tears from my eyes)”.      Following the superb “On Bond street” (2004), “MOVIN’ ON”, his new album, produced in Kingston with Reggae legends [Sly & Robbie](http://www.last.fm/music/Sly+&+Robbie), is scheduled for release in 2008.      McLean has also worked with the popular British reggae band, [UB40](http://www.last.fm/music/UB40), producing and mixing their “Promises and Lies” best seller and participated in “Running Free” Ali Campbell’s solo album.
