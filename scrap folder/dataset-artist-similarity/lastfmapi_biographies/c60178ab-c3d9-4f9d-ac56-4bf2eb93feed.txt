Linval Thompson is a [reggae](http://www.last.fm/tag/reggae) and [dub](http://www.last.fm/tag/dub) artist and producer.      He started life writing his own music and was recognised to have great talent not only with songwriting but also singing. He worked at [Lee “Scratch” Perry](http://www.last.fm/music/Lee+%22Scratch%22+Perry)’s Black Ark studios and contributed to [The Upsetters](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Upsetters) classic “Kung Fu Man”. Although he found greatest acclaim as a producer between the mid-70s and mid-80s, working with greats such as [Scientist](http://www.last.fm/music/Scientist), [King Tubby](http://www.last.fm/music/King+Tubby) and singer [Johnny Clarke](http://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Clarke). He has produced albums for artists such as [Dennis Brown](http://www.last.fm/music/Dennis+Brown), [The Wailing Souls](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Wailing+Souls), [Eek-a-Mouse](http://www.last.fm/music/Eek-a-Mouse), [Freddie McGregor](http://www.last.fm/music/Freddie+McGregor), and [The Viceroys](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Viceroys).
