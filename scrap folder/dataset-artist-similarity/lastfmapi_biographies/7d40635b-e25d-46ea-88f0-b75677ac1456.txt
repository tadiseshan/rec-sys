**Mikael Wiehe** (born 10 April 1946) is a Swedish [singer-songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag/singer-songwriter) and multi-instrumentalist. Throughout his career, his lyrical content has chiefly dealt with social and political issues from a leftist viewpoint. In the 1970s, Wiehe was one of the lead singers and the leading songwriter of [Hoola Bandoola Band](http://www.last.fm/music/Hoola+Bandoola+Band).      After the split of the group in 1975, he was the leader of various other bands before becoming a solo artist in the beginning of the 1980s.
