Orup (Thomas Eriksson) born december 29th, 1958 in Stockholm is a Swedish pop artist that has enjoyed major success in [Sweden](http://www.last.fm/place/Sweden) in the late 1980’s with songs like “Då står pojkarna på rad”, “Magaluf” and “Regn hos mig”. Orup is also a songwriter and has written songs for [Lena Philipsson](http://www.last.fm/music/Lena+Philipsson) among others.      Orup was also in [Intermezzo](http://www.last.fm/music/Intermezzo), [Ubangi](http://www.last.fm/music/Ubangi) (with [Cia Berg](http://www.last.fm/music/Cia+Berg)), the ‘E’ in [GES](http://www.last.fm/music/GES) and wrote songs for the kids tv-show Bröderna Olsson in the 80s.
