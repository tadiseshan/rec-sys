Taio Cruz (pronounced Tie-oh Cruise) (born 23 April 1983) is a Brit Award winning and MOBO-nominated English [singer-songwriter](http://www.last.fm/tag /singer-songwriter), occasional [rapper](http://www.last.fm/tag/rapper), and [music producer](http://www.last.fm/tag/music%20producer).      In 2008 he released his debut album [Departure](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/Departure) which he arranged and produced himself. In October 2009, he released his follow up album [Rokstarr](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/Rokstarr) which includes his number one single “[Break Your Heart](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/_/Break+Your+Heart)”. In between the two albums and at present, Cruz has been working on productions and writing songs for [Cheryl Cole](http://www.last.fm/music/Cheryl+Cole), [Brandy](http://www.last.fm/music/Brandy) and the [Sugababes](http://www.last.fm/music/Sugababes).      He is the founder and C.E.O. of Rokstarr Music London, which in 2006 released his debut single “[I Just Wanna Know](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/_/I+Just+Wanna+Know)”. Also in 2006, he signed a joint-venture partnership deal with Music Companies, [Republic Records](http://www.last.fm/label/Republic+Records) and [Island Records](http://www.last.fm/label/Island+Records). In the UK, Cruz has worked with many artists including [Omar](http://www.last.fm/music/Omar), [Nitin Sawhney](http://www.last.fm/music/Nitin+Sawhney), [Wookie](http://www.last.fm/music/Wookie), and [Will Young](http://www.last.fm/music/Will+Young).      Following the success of his own singles, record label chief Simon Cowell approached Cruz to write and produce tracks for [Alexandra Burke](http://www.last.fm/music/Alexandra+Burke), [Leona Lewis](http://www.last.fm/music/Leona+Lewis) and [Shayne Ward](http://www.last.fm/music/Shayne+Ward).      At the 2008 MOBO Awards he was nominated for best single for the song “[She’s Like a Star](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/_/She%27s+Like+a+Star)” and performed the single alongside the [Sugababes](http://www.last.fm/music/Sugababes) who feature on the official remix of the song.      In April 2010 Taio Cruz performed a live music session for London’s [Big Top 40](http://www.bigtop40.com/) with presenters Rich and Kat. An audience of influential UK bloggers were present in the Big Top 40 studios, during an intimate, live acoustic performance. The artist was interviewed by Rich and Kat and he also performed three songs from his second album, [Rokstarr](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/Rokstarr), including [Dirty Picture](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/_/Dirty+Picture) and [Break Your Heart](http://www.last.fm/music/Taio+Cruz/_/Break+Your+Heart). During his interview Taio Cruz told Rich and Kat that [Michael Jackson](http://www.last.fm/music/Michael+Jackson) would be his dream collaborator as well as [Beyoncé](http://www.last.fm/music/Beyonc%C3%A9) and [Justin Timberlake](http://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Timberlake)
