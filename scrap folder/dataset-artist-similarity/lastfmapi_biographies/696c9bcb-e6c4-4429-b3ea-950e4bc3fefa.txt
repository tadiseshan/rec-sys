[2Mex](http://www.last.fm/music/2Mex) (b. Alejandro Ocana) is an underground [hiphop](http://www.last.fm/tag/hiphop) producer and MC from Mid-City Los Angeles, California. He started out as one half of the duo [Of Mexican Descent](http://www.last.fm/music/Of+Mexican+Descent) (OMD) with [Xololanxinxo](http://www.last.fm/music/Xololanxinxo).      He has released numerous albums as solo projects and as a collaborations. He is a member of the [Visionaries](http://www.last.fm/music/Visionaries), a group formed by [Key kool](http://www.last.fm/music/Key+kool) and [DJ Rhettmatic](http://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Rhettmatic). He has also contributed to the efforts of [Project Blowed](http://www.last.fm/group/Project+Blowed), Afterlife Crew, the Mind Clouders, and [$martyr](http://www.last.fm/music/%24martyr).      2Mexâ€™s has made guest appearances on albums by [Atmosphere](http://www.last.fm/music/Atmosphere), [el vuh](http://www.last.fm/music/el+vuh), Abstract Rude, and [Busdriver](http://www.last.fm/music/Busdriver). He also performs and produces under the moniker SunGodSons.
