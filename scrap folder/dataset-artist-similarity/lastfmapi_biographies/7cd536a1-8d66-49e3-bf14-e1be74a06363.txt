**Stacey Kent** (born March 27, 1968 in South Orange, New Jersey) is a Grammy-nominated USA [jazz](http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz) vocalist, who debuted with _”[Close Your Eyes](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Close+Your+Eyes)”_ (1997) and has _”[Hushabye Mountain](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Hushabye+Mountain)”_ (2011), as her latest album.      Kent attended Newark Academy in Livingston, New Jersey. She graduated with a Degree in Comparative Literature from Sarah Lawrence College in New York, and moved to England after her graduation. While studying at London’s Guildhall School of Music and Drama, she met tenor saxophonist, **[Jim Tomlinson](http://www.last.fm/music/Jim+Tomlinson)**, whom she married on 9 Aug 1991.      **Discography**:    _”[Close Your Eyes](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Close+Your+Eyes)”_ (1997), _”[The Tender Trap](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/The+Tender+Trap)”_ (1998), _”[Let Yourself Go: Celebrating Fred Astaire](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Le t+Yourself+Go:+Celebrating+Fred+Astaire)”_ (2000),   _”[Dreamsville](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Dreamsville)”_ (2001), _”[Brazilian Sketches](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Brazilian+Sketches)”_ (2001), _”[In Love Again: The Music of Richard Rodgers](http://www.last.fm/music/Stace y+Kent/In+Love+Again:+The+Music+of+Richard+Rodgers)”_ (2002),   _”[The Boy Next Door](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/The+Boy+Next+Door)”_ (2003), _”[Collection](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Collection)”_ (2001), _”[Collection II](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Collection+II)”_ (2003), _”[Collection III](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Collection+III)”_ (2006),   _”[Breakfast On The Morning Tram](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Breakfast+On+The+Morning+Tram)”_ (2007), _”[Breakfast On The Morning Tram](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Breakfast+On+The+Morning+Tram)”_ (2008, limited edition boxset CD/DVD),   _”[Raconte-Moi…](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Raconte-Moi...)”_ (2010) , _”[Hushabye Mountain](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/Hushabye+Mountain)”_ (2011) , _”[The Changing Lights](http://www.last.fm/music/Stacey+Kent/The+Changing+Lights)”_ (2013)         Kent has also featured in Tomlinson’s 2006 album, _[The Lyric](http://www.last.fm/music/Jim+Tomlinson/The+Lyric)_, which won “Album of the Year” at the 2006 BBC Jazz Awards.      **Awards**: British Jazz Award (2001) and BBC Jazz Award for Best Vocalist (2002).      Kent’s album, _Breakfast on the Morning Tram_, was a nominee for Best Jazz Vocal Album at the 51st Grammy Awards (2009).      **Trivia**: Novelist [Kazuo Ishiguro](http://www.last.fm/music/Kazuo+Ishiguro) wrote the liner notes to Kent’s 2003 album, _The Boy Next Door_, and also co-wrote four of the songs on the 2007 album, _Breakfast on the Morning Tram_.
