Natasha Thomas is a Danish Singer who was born on September 27th 1986. Her style of music is pop and R&B. She started singing at the age of 12 and at 16 she released her first single Why (Does Your Love Hurt So Much) which was a top single for her. She then released her very first album Save Your Kisses For Me (2004), She then released a 2nd single called It’s Over Now, not long after she appeared in the advertisements for Lacoste’s new perfume Touch of Pink, which she sang ‘Skin Deep’ for. ‘It’s Over Now’ also featured on a Lacoste advert. She has now released her new album called Playing With Fire…
