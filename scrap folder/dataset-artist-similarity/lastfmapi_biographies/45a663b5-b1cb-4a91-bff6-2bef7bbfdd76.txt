**Britney Jean Spears** (born on December 2, 1981 in Kentwood, LA, United States, and raised in Kentwood, Louisiana, United States) is a Grammy-winning multi-platinum American [pop](http://www.last.fm/tag/pop) singer, songwriter, dancer and occasional actress. She debuted in 1998 with her single […Baby One More Time](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/_/...Baby+One+More+Time). She is one of the most influential and popular post-2000 icons of music. Spears is one of the few young musicians to appear on lists of best selling music acts of all-time. She also holds several world records.      She has officially released eight studio albums to date:”[…Baby One More Time](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/...Baby+One+More+Time)” (1999), “[Oops!…I Did It Again](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Oops!...I+Did+It+Again)” (2000), “[Britney](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Britney)” (2001), “[In the Zone](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/In+the+Zone)” (2003), “[Blackout](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Blackout)” (2007), “[Circus](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Circus)” (2008), “[Femme Fatale](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Femme+Fatale)” (2011), “[Britney Jean](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Britney+Jean)”(2013).      Along with 2 official compilation albums, 2 official remixes albums and 1 official extended play: “[Greatest Hits: My Prerogative](http://www.last.fm/mu sic/Britney+Spears/Greatest+Hits:+My+Prerogative)” (2004), “[B in the Mix: The Remixes](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/B+in+the+Mix:+The+Remixes)” (2005), “[Britney & Kevin: Chaotic](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/Britney+&+Kevin:+Chaotic)” (2005), “[The Singles Collection](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/The+Singles+Collection)” (2009) and “[B in the Mix: The Remixes Vol. 2](http://www.last.fm/music/Britney+Spears/B+in+the+Mix:+The+Remixes+Vol.+2)” (2011).      As a child, Spears performed as a cast member of _The New Mickey Mouse Club_ from 1993 to 1994, along with [Justin Timberlake](http://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Timberlake) and [Christina Aguilera](http://www.last.fm/music/Christina+Aguilera).      In September 2004, she married Kevin Federline. The couple had two sons, Sean (born September 2005) and Jayden (born September 2006). Spears filed for divorce in November 2006.      Britney’s debut album’ …Baby One More Time’ was a huge commercial success. It debuted at number one in the U.S. with 120,500 copies sold in its first week on the Billboard 200, where it remained for 6 non-consecutive weeks, and it also debuted at number one in Canada, and reached the top ten in many other countries. The record has been certified 14× Platinum in the United States. It is the highest selling album by a teenager both in the US and worldwide.
