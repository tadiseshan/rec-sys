Aaron Neville (born January 24, 1941, New Orleans, Louisiana) is an American soul and R&B singer and musician. He made his debut in 1966 with the hit single [Tell It Like It Is](http://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Neville/_/Tell+It+Like+It+Is), a Number One hit on the Billboard R&B charts. From 1977 he performed as part of [The Neville Brothers](http://www.last.fm/music/The+Neville+Brothers) with his siblings, [Art Neville](http://www.last.fm/music/Art+Neville), [Charles Neville](http://www.last.fm/music/Charles+Neville), [Cyril Neville](http://www.last.fm/music/Cyril+Neville) & [Ivan Neville](http://www.last.fm/music/Ivan+Neville).      Neville charted again, in 1989, when he collaborated with [Linda Ronstadt](http://www.last.fm/music/Linda+Ronstadt) on three consecutive duets: [Don’t Know Much](http://www.last.fm/music/Linda+Ronstadt+&+Aaron+Neville/_/Do n%27t+Know+Much), [All My Life](http://www.last.fm/music/Linda+Ronstadt+&+Aaron+Neville/_/All+My+Life), and [When Something Is Wrong With My Baby](http://www.last.fm/music/Linda+Rons tadt+&+Aaron+Neville/_/When+Something+Is+Wrong+With+My+Baby), the first two reached Number One on the Adult Contemporary charts. Neville has released more than 20 singles, including three Number Ones on the Adult Contemporary format, and a fourth on the R&B format.      His other hits have included [Everybody Plays The Fool](http://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Neville/_/Everybody+Plays+The+Fool), his successful 1991 cover of the 1972 Main Ingredient song, that reached #8 on the Hot 100; [Don’t Take Away My Heaven](http://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Neville/_/Don%27t+Take+Away+My+Heaven), [Hercules](http://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Neville/_/Hercules) and [Can’t Stop My Heart From Loving You (The Rain Song)](http://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Nevil le/_/Can%27t+Stop+My+Heart+From+Loving+You+\(The+Rain+Song\)). Neville’s biggest solo successes have been on the Adult Contemporary chart, where “Don’t Know Much,” “All My Life,” and “Everybody Plays the Fool” all reached Number One.      Aaron signed to [SONYBMG](http://www.last.fm/label/SONYBMG)’s new Burgundy Records label in late 2005 and recorded an album of songs by [Otis Redding](http://www.last.fm/music/Otis+Redding), [Marvin Gaye](http://www.last.fm/music/Marvin+Gaye), [Curtis Mayfield](http://www.last.fm/music/Curtis+Mayfield), [Sam Cooke](http://www.last.fm/music/Sam+Cooke) and others for [Bring It On Home…The Soul Classics](http://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Neville/Bring+It+On+Hom e...The+Soul+Classics), released on September 19, 2006. The album, produced by Stewart Levine, features collaborations between Neville and [Chaka Khan](http://www.last.fm/music/Chaka+Khan), [Mavis Staples](http://www.last.fm/music/Mavis+Staples), [Chris Botti](http://www.last.fm/music/Chris+Botti), [David Sanborn](http://www.last.fm/music/David+Sanborn), [Art Neville](http://www.last.fm/music/Art+Neville), and others
